# Episode 7 - The Stamp Hunt That Got Weird

## Type
Campus exploration / comedy adventure / clue escalation

## Main characters
- Muci
- Padaruna
- Nia
- Snacri
- Sunny Spritz

## Episode promise
What looks like a standard new-student scavenger game turns into the group's first real campus investigation as old maps, replaced plaques, and founder-era traces begin lining up.

## Core plot
The school launches a Campus Stamp Hunt meant to familiarize new students with iconic locations. For the protagonists, it becomes the perfect excuse to compare the official route to the older map. They pass Founder Walk, Ingredient Library, Student Store, Repair Clinic, and the outside of Barrier Sciences Hall.

Padaruna treats the event like a speedrun challenge. Nia reduces it to route optimization. Muci is the one actually scanning the space for mismatches. At Founder Walk, they notice that a plaque has been physically replaced, and beneath the newer installation they can make out the old words “Glow Thesis.”

This is the first moment the mystery shifts from altered paper to altered campus architecture.

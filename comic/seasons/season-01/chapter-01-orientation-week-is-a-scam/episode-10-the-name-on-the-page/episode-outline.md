# Episode 10 - The Name on the Page

## Type
Chapter cliffhanger / archive reveal / mystery escalation

## Main characters
- Muci
- Padaruna
- Snacri
- Artrans

## Episode promise
The first real fragment of The Glow Thesis reframes the entire school philosophy and confirms that the deeper conflict is not about finding a perfect routine, but about who controls meaning.

## Core plot
The toppled files reveal a yellowed research page rather than a formula sheet. The language is philosophical, not instructional. The clearest surviving sentence is: “There is no universal glow.” That line collides directly with everything the modern institution has been implying about discipline, standard answers, and idealized skin outcomes.

Padaruna is stunned that a secret skincare document would reject certainty instead of offering it. Muci realizes the old school may have believed something much kinder and more complex than the current one. Artrans visibly loses his usual ease and finally admits that the page is part of The Glow Thesis.

When Muci turns the page over, he sees the ring insignia again. Artrans names it in a low voice: The Luster Circle.

## Skincare knowledge
- There is no universal routine that works for everyone.
- Skincare is not about chasing a single perfect answer.
- Understanding individual variation matters more than standardization.

## American humor beats
- Padaruna's disbelief gives the reveal a grounded, human reaction instead of melodrama.

## Foreshadowing
- The Luster Circle is formally named.
- The school's official philosophy may have been built against the original thesis.
- Other people may already be searching for the remaining fragments.

## Ending hook
Artrans warns that if this page was left here, someone else is already hunting the rest.

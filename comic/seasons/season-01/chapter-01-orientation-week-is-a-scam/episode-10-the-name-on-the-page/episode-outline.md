# Episode 10 - The Fragment Behind the Wall

## 中文

### 类型
隐藏档案 / The Glow Thesis 碎片 / Chapter 收束钩子

### 主要角色
- Muci
- Nia
- Padarana

### Episode 承诺
三人在墙后的隐藏区域找到 The Glow Thesis 的第一块碎片。它不是配方，也不是完美 routine，而是一段反对标准答案的旧研究/哲学文字；活动时间快结束时，他们只能匆忙带着碎片返回。

### 核心剧情
旧 handbook 停在 Observation Archive 深处的一张窄桌上。桌面没有整齐档案，只有被翻乱的纸页、空白索引卡和几处像是最近才被拿走东西的干净印痕。Muci 注意到其中一处印痕正好是旧 handbook 的大小，像这本书原本就属于这里。

Nia 开始快速拍照和记录，试图在夜晚活动结束前把线索整理清楚。Padarana 在一排旧抽屉后面发现一张被夹住的残页。残页纸张发黄，边缘缺失，文字不像产品说明，更像研究笔记或理念手稿。最清楚的一句是：There is no universal glow.

这句话让三人第一次理解 The Glow Thesis 的方向：它不是教大家追求同一个完美答案，而是在强调个体差异、长期观察和不被标准化叙事吞掉。Muci 把残页靠近旧 handbook，书页微微发亮，像在回应缺失的一部分。Nia 发现残页背面有与旧地图、旧书签、旧铭牌一致的被刮除环形印记，继续匹配固定物件 `Scratched Ring Mark.jpg`。

Padarana 听到远处活动广播提醒所有学生回到集合点。三人没有时间继续翻找。Nia 判断残页留在这里并不安全，Muci 小心把它夹进旧 handbook 里，Padarana 负责把现场恢复到不会立刻暴露的状态。

他们沿原路匆忙返回。穿墙时，隐藏走廊的灯一盏盏熄灭，墙面恢复成普通粉刷墙。三人赶回集合点时还在喘气，只能先假装夜晚活动一切正常。Muci 的旧 handbook 却在包里发出很微弱的光，证明他们带回的不只是纸，而是一条主线。

### 护肤知识
- 没有适用于所有人的 universal routine。
- 护肤不是追逐唯一完美答案，而是观察自己的真实反应。
- 标准化说明有用，但不能替代个体差异和长期记录。

### 连续性锁定
- 本话只让 Muci、Nia 和 Padarana 参与核心发现与对话。
- The Glow Thesis fragment 是后续章节反复使用的主线物件，必须保持黄旧纸张、缺页边缘、研究/哲学手稿感。
- 残页背面的被刮除环形印记必须匹配 `Scratched Ring Mark.jpg`。
- 结尾必须保留“活动时间结束，他们匆忙带着碎片回去报告/集合”的节奏。

### 美式喜剧点
- Nia 在最紧张的时候还试图给每张照片编号。
- Muci 小心夹残页时像在处理会投诉他的图书馆资料。
- Padarana 恢复现场的方式过于专业，自己也停顿一秒说：“这可能不是普通学生技能。”

### 伏笔
- The Glow Thesis 正式成为主线物件，但只出现残片，不给完整答案。
- 环形印记与隐藏区域、旧 handbook 和旧校园路线形成同一条线。
- 有人可能比他们更早来过，并拿走了其他碎片。

### 结尾钩子
回到集合点后，Muci 打开包确认残页还在。残页背面的环形印记在昏暗光线里短暂发亮，旁边浮出几乎看不清的一行旧字：Luster Circle.

## English

### Type
Hidden archive / The Glow Thesis fragment / chapter-closing hook

### Main Characters
- Muci
- Nia
- Padarana

### Episode Promise
Behind the wall, the trio finds the first fragment of The Glow Thesis. It is not a formula or a perfect routine, but an old research/philosophy note rejecting standard answers. When the activity time is almost over, they rush back with the fragment.

### Core Plot
The old handbook stops on a narrow table deep inside the Observation Archive. The table does not hold neat files. It holds scattered pages, blank index cards, and a few clean marks where objects seem to have been removed recently. Muci notices that one empty mark is exactly the size of the old handbook, as if the book originally belonged here.

Nia photographs and documents as quickly as possible before the night activity ends. Padarana finds a page fragment caught behind a row of old drawers. The paper is yellowed, torn at the edges, and written less like product instructions than a research or philosophy manuscript. The clearest surviving sentence reads: There is no universal glow.

For the first time, the trio understands the direction of The Glow Thesis. It does not teach everyone to chase the same perfect answer. It values individual difference, long-term observation, and refusing to be swallowed by standardized language. When Muci brings the fragment near the old handbook, the pages glow faintly, as if recognizing a missing part. Nia finds the same scraped ring mark on the back of the fragment, matching the old map, bookmark, plaque traces, and fixed prop `Scratched Ring Mark.jpg`.

Padarana hears a distant activity announcement telling students to return to the meeting point. They do not have time to keep searching. Nia decides the fragment is not safe here. Muci carefully places it inside the old handbook, while Padarana restores the scene enough that the discovery will not be obvious immediately.

They hurry back through the same route. As they pass through the wall, the hidden corridor lights shut off one by one, and the wall returns to plain painted surface. They reach the meeting point out of breath and pretend the night activity was completely normal. Inside Muci's bag, the old handbook gives off a faint glow, proving they brought back more than paper. They brought back the chapter's main thread.

### Skincare Knowledge
- There is no universal routine that works for everyone.
- Skincare is not about chasing one perfect answer; it is about observing real personal response.
- Standard instructions can help, but they cannot replace individual differences and long-term records.

### Continuity Locks
- Only Muci, Nia, and Padarana participate in the core discovery and dialogue.
- The Glow Thesis fragment is a recurring main-story object; preserve the yellowed paper, torn edge, and research/philosophy manuscript feel.
- The scraped ring mark on the fragment back must match `Scratched Ring Mark.jpg`.
- The ending must preserve the rhythm of activity time running out and the trio rushing back with the fragment to report or rejoin the group.

### American Humor Beats
- Nia still tries to number every photo during the most stressful moment.
- Muci handles the fragment like library material that might file a complaint.
- Padarana restores the scene a little too professionally, then pauses and says, "That might not be a normal student skill."

### Foreshadowing
- The Glow Thesis formally becomes a main-thread object, but only as a fragment, not a full answer.
- The ring mark links the hidden area, old handbook, old campus route, and fragment into one clue chain.
- Someone may have arrived earlier and removed the other fragments.

### Ending Hook
Back at the meeting point, Muci opens his bag to confirm the fragment is still there. In the dim light, the ring mark on the back briefly glows, and a nearly unreadable old phrase appears beside it: Luster Circle.

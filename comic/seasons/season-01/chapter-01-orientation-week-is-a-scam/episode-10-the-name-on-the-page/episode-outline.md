# Episode 10 - The Name on the Page

## 中文

### 类型
Chapter cliffhanger / 档案揭示 / 谜团升级

### 主要角色
- Muci
- Padaruna
- Snacri
- Artrans

### Episode 承诺
Chapter 1 的第一个真正答案出现：The Glow Thesis 不是配方，也不是完美 routine，而是一套反对标准答案的旧理念。本话把前面所有旧句子、旧地图、旧物件和环形符号连成主线。

### 核心剧情
Muci 撞倒的文件里没有神秘配方表，而是一页泛黄的研究/哲学手稿。最清楚的一句是：“There is no universal glow.” 这句话直接撞上现代 Neatique 一直强调的纪律、标准答案和理想状态。

Padaruna 惊讶于一个秘密护肤文件竟然不是给出更强规则，而是否定唯一答案。Snacri 快速指出页面边缘的编号缺失，说明这只是残页。Artrans 终于承认这属于 The Glow Thesis，但他仍然不愿说自己为什么知道。

Muci 翻过纸页，看到和旧铭牌、书签相同的环形符号。Artrans 低声说出名字：The Luster Circle。这个名字让 Chapter 1 从校园异常进入更大的组织谜团。

### 护肤知识
- 没有适用于所有人的 universal routine。
- 护肤不是追逐单一完美答案。
- 个体差异和长期观察比标准化结果更重要。

### 连续性锁定
- The Glow Thesis 残页、环形符号、旧文件堆和 reading room 必须作为后续主线物件/场景保持一致。
- 不要把残页画成公式或产品说明；它应该更像哲学/研究手稿。

### 美式喜剧点
- Padaruna 对“秘密文件居然不提供终极规则”感到真诚失望。
- Snacri 在所有人震惊时已经开始数页码。
- Artrans 试图继续装懒，但整个人明显失败。

### 伏笔
- The Luster Circle 正式被命名。
- 学校现在的官方理念可能是为了反制原始 thesis。
- 还有其他人正在寻找剩下的残页。

### 结尾钩子
Artrans 警告他们：如果这页被故意留在这里，那说明有人已经在找剩下的部分。

## English

### Type
Chapter cliffhanger / archive reveal / mystery escalation

### Main Characters
- Muci
- Padaruna
- Snacri
- Artrans

### Episode Promise
Chapter 1 delivers its first real answer: The Glow Thesis is not a formula or a perfect routine, but an older philosophy against standard answers. The episode connects the old sentences, old maps, old objects, and ring symbol into the main mystery.

### Core Plot
The files Muci knocks over do not reveal a secret formula sheet, but a yellowed research/philosophy page. The clearest surviving sentence is: “There is no universal glow.” That line collides directly with modern Neatique's emphasis on discipline, standard answers, and idealized outcomes.

Padaruna is genuinely shocked that a secret skincare document rejects one final rule instead of providing a stronger one. Snacri immediately notices missing page numbers, proving this is only a fragment. Artrans finally admits that the page belongs to The Glow Thesis, though he still refuses to explain why he knows.

Muci turns the page over and sees the same ring symbol from the old plaque and bookmark. Artrans names it quietly: The Luster Circle. That name moves Chapter 1 from campus oddities into a larger organizational mystery.

### Skincare Knowledge
- There is no universal routine that works for everyone.
- Skincare is not about chasing one perfect answer.
- Individual variation and long-term observation matter more than standardized outcomes.

### Continuity Locks
- The Glow Thesis fragment, ring symbol, old file stack, and reading room must remain consistent as recurring main-story objects and spaces.
- Do not depict the fragment as a formula or product instruction sheet; it should feel like a philosophical/research manuscript.

### American Humor Beats
- Padaruna is sincerely disappointed that the secret document does not provide the ultimate rule.
- Snacri starts counting missing page numbers while everyone else is shocked.
- Artrans tries to stay lazy and detached, but visibly fails.

### Foreshadowing
- The Luster Circle is formally named.
- The school's current philosophy may exist in opposition to the original thesis.
- Other people are already searching for the remaining fragments.

### Ending Hook
Artrans warns them: if this page was deliberately left here, someone is already hunting for the rest.

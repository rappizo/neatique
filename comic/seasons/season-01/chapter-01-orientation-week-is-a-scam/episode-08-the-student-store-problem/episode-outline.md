# Episode 8 - The Day Map Says Otherwise

## 中文

### 类型
白天校园探查 / 地图差异 / 物件线索

### 主要角色
- Muci
- Nia
- Padarana

### Episode 承诺
三人把 Student Store 里发现的旧标语、旧物件和最新学生手册放在一起核对，发现校园白天看似正常的路线其实被重新命名、重新粉刷、重新解释过。故事从“奇怪商品”推进到“学校空间本身被改写”。

### 核心剧情
白天的学校探查活动开始后，Muci、Nia 和 Padarana 被分到同一条校园路线。Nia 坚持用最新学生手册做基准，Muci 拿着旧地图和旧书签对照，Padarana 负责观察那些“看起来不重要但很不对劲”的现场细节。

他们先回到 Student Store 外侧，发现店内商品标语使用的是最新手册里的标准说法，但货架背面的旧贴纸、收银台下方的旧标签和折扣书签都使用另一套语言。旧书签边缘的环形痕迹必须与固定物件 `Scratched Ring Mark.jpg` 保持一致，像是被人刻意刮掉后又残留下来。

三人继续沿着旧地图走。旧地图上的 Founder Walk 在最新手册里被改成了 Wellness Loop，旧铭牌的位置被新标识盖住，但墙面和地砖还留下拆除后的轮廓。Muci 发现旧地图的路线并没有消失，只是每个入口都被新的说明牌温和地改成了“无关区域”。Nia 越查越生气，因为这些差异不像普通更新，而像系统性改写。

Padarana 在一面重新粉刷过的墙边发现细小的旧铭牌边缘，和书签、旧地图上的符号方向一致。三人意识到这些旧物件不是散落的怀旧物，而是在把他们引向一条被学校白天活动刻意绕开的路线。

### 护肤知识
- 最新包装和最新说明不一定等于事实完整。
- 真正有用的信息需要长期观察，而不是只看被整理过的版本。
- 同一件事被重新命名后，使用者很容易忘记它原本的功能。

### 连续性锁定
- 本话只让 Muci、Nia 和 Padarana 推动事件与对话，不加入 Snacri、Artrans 或其他角色共同探查。
- 旧地图、最新学生手册、旧书签、旧铭牌痕迹和 `Scratched Ring Mark.jpg` 环形印记必须作为同一条线索使用。
- 白天路线必须为 Episode 9 夜晚重新探查提供清楚前因。

### 美式喜剧点
- Nia 对“版本不一致”的愤怒比对神秘事件本身更强烈。
- Muci 每次想把事情解释成普通维修，下一秒都会看到更明显的异常。
- Padarana 用很轻松的语气说出很可怕的观察，比如“这面墙看起来像刚学会撒谎。”

### 伏笔
- 旧地图和最新手册在同一条走廊终点产生冲突。
- 环形印记不是装饰，而是某个旧组织或旧理念留下的标记。
- 被改写的校园路线在夜晚可能会出现不同结果。

### 结尾钩子
Nia 指着旧地图上一个在最新手册里完全消失的转角，说他们白天不能硬闯，但夜晚活动会经过附近。她决定晚上按 Old Version 的 handbook 再走一遍。

## English

### Type
Daytime campus investigation / map discrepancy / object clue

### Main Characters
- Muci
- Nia
- Padarana

### Episode Promise
The trio compares old slogans, old objects, and the latest student handbook, discovering that ordinary campus spaces have been renamed, repainted, and re-explained. The mystery moves from strange merchandise to the school grounds themselves being edited.

### Core Plot
When the daytime investigation activity begins, Muci, Nia, and Padarana are assigned the same campus route. Nia treats the latest student handbook as the official baseline, Muci compares it with the old map and bookmark, and Padarana watches for small physical details that look unimportant but feel wrong.

They start near the Student Store exterior. The current displays use the latest handbook language, but old shelf stickers, register labels, and discount bookmarks use a different vocabulary. The ring scar on the old bookmark must match the fixed prop `Scratched Ring Mark.jpg`, as if the mark had been deliberately scraped away but never fully removed.

The trio follows the old map. Founder Walk has become Wellness Loop in the latest handbook. Old plaque locations are covered by new signage, but the wall and floor still show removal outlines. Muci realizes the old route has not disappeared; every entrance has simply been softened into an official "unrelated area." Nia grows increasingly angry because the differences feel less like updates and more like systematic rewriting.

Padarana finds a thin old-plaque edge beside a freshly painted wall, aligned with the bookmark and old-map symbols. The group realizes the old objects are not random nostalgia. They are steering them toward a route the daytime school activity carefully avoids.

### Skincare Knowledge
- New packaging and new instructions do not always mean the full truth is present.
- Useful information depends on long-term observation, not only curated versions.
- Once something is renamed, people can forget what it originally did.

### Continuity Locks
- Only Muci, Nia, and Padarana drive the event and dialogue in this episode; do not add Snacri, Artrans, or other characters to the investigation.
- The old map, latest student handbook, old bookmark, old plaque traces, and `Scratched Ring Mark.jpg` ring mark must function as one clue chain.
- The daytime route must clearly set up Episode 9's night investigation.

### American Humor Beats
- Nia is more offended by version inconsistency than by the supernatural implications.
- Every time Muci tries to explain something as normal maintenance, a bigger contradiction appears.
- Padarana says alarming observations lightly, such as: "This wall looks like it just learned how to lie."

### Foreshadowing
- The old map and latest handbook disagree at the end of the same corridor.
- The ring mark is not decoration; it belongs to an older group or philosophy.
- The rewritten route may behave differently at night.

### Ending Hook
Nia points to a corner that exists on the old map but has vanished from the latest handbook. They cannot force it during the day, but the night activity passes nearby. She decides they should follow the Old Version handbook route after dark.

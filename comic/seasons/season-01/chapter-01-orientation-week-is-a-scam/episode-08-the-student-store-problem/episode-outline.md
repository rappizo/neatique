# Episode 8 - The Student Store Problem

## 中文

### 类型
校园商店喜剧 / 物件线索 / 历史漂移

### 主要角色
- Muci
- Padaruna
- Snacri
- Tuck Trinket
- Nia

### Episode 承诺
一次看似轻松的校园商店采购，把旧标语和旧物件重新带到读者眼前。小组开始意识到：学校历史不是自然消失，而是通过商品、库存、话术和展示方式被持续管理。

### 核心剧情
Padaruna 拉大家去 Student Store 买学校限定用品，并坚持自己只是“理性考察”，不是被营销影响。商店像奢华童话精品店，到处都是 founder-themed goods、抛光展示和情绪价值极高的校园周边。

店员 Tuck Trinket 用完美客服笑容说出许多不该随口知道的旧产品线信息。Snacri 在折扣篮里找到一批旧书签，上面写着 “Glow begins with observation.” Nia 注意到库存系统显示这些书签早该下架，Padaruna 则先被它们的设计美学吸引。

Muci 把书签边缘的环形符号和 Hydration Greenhouse 旧铭牌背面的痕迹对上。旧物件不是随机出现，它们像是在把小组往某个方向引。

### 护肤知识
- 包装和 hype 不等于真实需求。
- 稀缺营销会扭曲判断。
- 因焦虑购买和因使用需要购买是两回事。

### 连续性锁定
- 旧书签、环形符号、Student Store 展示架如果后续继续出现，需要统一物件参考。
- 不要重复角色首次登场框。

### 美式喜剧点
- Padaruna 坚称自己不受营销影响，同时完全被营销影响。
- Tuck 用客服语气说出高度可疑的信息。
- Nia 对库存错误的愤怒比对谜团本身更直接。

### 伏笔
- 旧物件正在通过库存系统重新出现。
- 环形符号和旧理念之间存在联系。

### 结尾钩子
书签背面有一行几乎被磨掉的小字：Look where the map refuses to go.

## English

### Type
Campus store comedy / object clue / history drift

### Main Characters
- Muci
- Padaruna
- Snacri
- Tuck Trinket
- Nia

### Episode Promise
A seemingly light campus-store trip brings old slogans and old objects back into view. The group begins to realize that school history has not naturally vanished; it is being managed through merchandise, inventory, language, and display.

### Core Plot
Padaruna drags everyone to the Student Store for school-exclusive supplies while insisting she is conducting “rational research,” not being influenced by marketing. The store feels like a luxury fairytale boutique, full of founder-themed goods, polished displays, and emotionally persuasive campus merchandise.

Tuck Trinket, the clerk, delivers suspiciously specific old product-line information with perfect customer-service cheer. Snacri finds old bookmarks in a discount bin reading “Glow begins with observation.” Nia notices that the inventory system says these bookmarks should have been discontinued, while Padaruna is first distracted by their design.

Muci connects the ring symbol on the bookmark edge to the scratched mark from the Hydration Greenhouse plaque. The old objects are not appearing randomly; they seem to be steering the group somewhere.

### Skincare Knowledge
- Packaging and hype are not the same as real need.
- Scarcity marketing can distort judgment.
- Buying from anxiety is different from buying for use.

### Continuity Locks
- If the old bookmark, ring symbol, or Student Store display returns later, unify those prop references before image generation.
- Do not repeat first-appearance intro boxes.

### American Humor Beats
- Padaruna insists she is immune to marketing while being completely influenced by it.
- Tuck delivers suspicious information in flawless customer-service voice.
- Nia is more directly offended by the inventory error than by the mystery itself.

### Foreshadowing
- Old objects are reappearing through the inventory system.
- The ring symbol is connected to the older philosophy.

### Ending Hook
On the back of the bookmark, nearly worn away, is a tiny line: Look where the map refuses to go.

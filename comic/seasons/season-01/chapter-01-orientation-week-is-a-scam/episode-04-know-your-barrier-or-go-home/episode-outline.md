# Episode 4 - Know Your Barrier or Go Home

## 中文

### 类型
第一堂课 / Padaruna 角色登场 / 屏障护理喜剧

### 主要角色
- Muci
- Padaruna
- Professor Cera Lin
- Nia
- background classmates

### Episode 承诺
第一堂正式课程把 Neatique 的护肤逻辑讲清楚，同时让 Padaruna 第一次正式登场。她用 “Try Every Bad Idea Beautifully” 的介绍框进入故事，把 Muci 的谨慎和 Nia 的正确都搅得更有喜剧张力。

### 核心剧情
Muci 走进名为 “Know Your Barrier or Go Home” 的课程，立刻觉得自己像误入高阶课堂。Professor Cera Lin 用过度护理、过度去角质、产品乱跳和即时效果焦虑讲清楚：看起来很努力的混乱，仍然会伤害皮肤屏障。

Padaruna 登场时像把课堂当成舞台。她并不是不聪明，而是太愿意试、太愿意把错误也做得漂亮。她连续提出几个“如果我这样做会不会更快”的方案，把 Professor Cera Lin 逼到优雅沉默，也让 Muci 看见 Neatique 不只有标准答案型学生。

课堂小组练习里，Nia 负责正确，Padaruna 负责把错误想象到极致，Muci 负责惊恐地学习。旧 handbook 又一次出现矛盾：它把 barrier 解释成“边界需要被听见”，而新版教材把它写成“边界需要被训练”。

### 护肤知识
- 屏障稳定是基础，不是锦上添花。
- 强度不等于有效。
- 总是需要修复伤害的 routine，本身就已经失败。

### 视觉与角色锁定
- Padaruna 第一次正式登场时使用介绍框：Padaruna - Try Every Bad Idea Beautifully。
- Padaruna 要有兴奋、漂亮、冲动但不恶意的能量。她不是笨，而是太相信体验和动能。

### 美式喜剧点
- 课程标题像威胁。
- Padaruna 把 skincare mistake 讲得像创业 pitch。
- Muci 夹在 Nia 的正确和 Padaruna 的热情之间。

### 伏笔
- 新旧教材对 barrier 的语言差异暗示学校理念被改写。
- Professor Cera Lin 对旧表述有短暂反应，但没有解释。

### 结尾钩子
课后，Muci 发现自己 handbook 里那句旧解释被新版教材完全替换掉了。

## English

### Type
First class / Padaruna introduction / barrier-care comedy

### Main Characters
- Muci
- Padaruna
- Professor Cera Lin
- Nia
- background classmates

### Episode Promise
The first real class clarifies Neatique's skincare logic while formally introducing Padaruna. She enters with the intro box “Try Every Bad Idea Beautifully,” disrupting both Muci's caution and Nia's correctness with comic energy.

### Core Plot
Muci walks into a course titled “Know Your Barrier or Go Home” and immediately feels underqualified. Professor Cera Lin uses over-care, over-exfoliation, product hopping, and instant-results anxiety to make one point clear: chaos disguised as effort can still damage the skin barrier.

Padaruna arrives as if class is a stage. She is not unintelligent; she is simply too willing to try, and too willing to make mistakes beautifully. She proposes several “would this work faster?” ideas, forcing Professor Cera Lin into elegant silence and showing Muci that Neatique students are not all answer-key types.

During a class exercise, Nia provides correctness, Padaruna imagines errors to their most dramatic conclusion, and Muci learns in mild terror. The old handbook creates another mismatch: it describes the barrier as “a boundary that needs to be heard,” while the new course material says “a boundary that needs to be trained.”

### Skincare Knowledge
- Barrier stability is the foundation, not the glamorous extra.
- Intensity is not the same as effectiveness.
- A routine that constantly requires damage control is already failing.

### Visual And Character Locks
- Use the first-appearance intro box: Padaruna - Try Every Bad Idea Beautifully.
- Padaruna should feel excited, stylish, impulsive, and never mean. She is not foolish; she trusts experience and momentum too much.

### American Humor Beats
- The course title sounds like a threat.
- Padaruna describes skincare mistakes like startup pitches.
- Muci is trapped between Nia's correctness and Padaruna's enthusiasm.

### Foreshadowing
- The old and new barrier language hints that the school's philosophy has been rewritten.
- Professor Cera Lin reacts briefly to the old phrasing but does not explain it.

### Ending Hook
After class, Muci realizes the old explanation in his handbook has been completely replaced in the new course material.

# Episode 9 - The Handbook Goes Through the Wall

## 中文

### 类型
夜晚校园探查 / 隐藏入口 / Chapter cliffhanger 前奏

### 主要角色
- Muci
- Nia
- Padarana

### Episode 承诺
夜晚活动让白天发现的路线差异变成真实入口。Nia 按 Old Version 的 handbook 带路，Muci 手里的旧手册突然发光并穿过墙，三人第一次进入学校隐藏区域。

### 核心剧情
夜晚校园探查活动开始后，Muci、Nia 和 Padarana 没有加入大队伍的热闹对话，而是安静地沿着白天标记好的路线移动。Nia 坚持这不是乱闯，而是“版本验证”；Muci 坚持这听起来仍然很像乱闯；Padarana 负责观察有没有人注意到他们偏离路线。

他们按照 Old Version 的 handbook 走到一个在最新手册里不存在的转角。旧地图显示这里应该有一条通道，但现实中只有一面刚粉刷过的墙。墙面很干净，太干净了，只有低角度光线下能看到被刮掉的旧铭牌边框和环形印记残痕。环形印记继续沿用固定物件 `Scratched Ring Mark.jpg` 的形状与刮除质感。

Nia 认为旧 handbook 的路线已经到终点，正准备拍照记录，Muci 怀里的旧手册却突然发光。它不是打开，而是像被墙后某个东西吸引一样自己飞出来，书页翻动，直接穿过墙面消失。

三人短暂愣住。Muci 很小声地问这算不算学校财产损坏。Nia 没有等他讲完，直接伸手碰墙，发现墙面像冷水一样让她的手穿过去。她带头冲过墙，Padarana 确认外面没人后跟上，Muci 最后抱着“这绝对不是正常夜游”的表情穿过去。

墙后不是空房间，而是一条被封存的旧走廊。灯光很弱，空气里有纸张和旧木头的味道，墙上还留着与 Student Store 旧标签相同的语言体系。飞进去的旧 handbook 停在走廊深处，像是在等他们继续跟上。

### 护肤知识
本话以悬疑、空间发现和节奏推进为主，护肤知识只轻轻保留在“版本验证”和“不要只相信官方整理后的说明”里，不做大段教学。

### 连续性锁定
- 本话只让 Muci、Nia 和 Padarana 参与核心事件与对话。
- 旧 handbook 发光、从 Muci 身边飞出、穿过墙，是本话必须保留的关键动作。
- 墙面上的被刮除环形印记必须匹配 `Scratched Ring Mark.jpg`，并延续 Episode 5 最后一页固定物件。
- 隐藏走廊需要为 Episode 10 发现 The Glow Thesis fragment 做准备。

### 美式喜剧点
- Nia 把潜入隐藏空间称为“版本验证流程”。
- Muci 在最不该关注规则的时候担心学校财产损坏。
- Padarana 认真检查有没有监控，然后说“好消息，墙比摄像头更奇怪。”

### 伏笔
- 旧 handbook 像是能回应隐藏区域，而不只是普通道具。
- 被刮除环形印记出现在墙面入口附近，说明它是入口线索的一部分。
- 隐藏走廊保留了与最新学校语言不同的旧体系。

### 结尾钩子
旧 handbook 在走廊尽头停下，自动翻到缺页位置。墙内的灯一盏盏微弱亮起，照出门牌上几乎看不清的字：Observation Archive。

## English

### Type
Night campus investigation / hidden entrance / pre-cliffhanger

### Main Characters
- Muci
- Nia
- Padarana

### Episode Promise
The night activity turns the daytime route discrepancy into a real entrance. Nia follows the Old Version handbook, Muci's old handbook suddenly glows and passes through a wall, and the trio enters the school's hidden area for the first time.

### Core Plot
When the night campus investigation begins, Muci, Nia, and Padarana avoid the noisy group conversation and quietly move along the route they marked during the day. Nia insists this is not sneaking around but "version verification." Muci insists that still sounds a lot like sneaking around. Padarana watches to see whether anyone notices them leaving the route.

They follow the Old Version handbook to a corner that does not exist in the latest handbook. The old map says there should be a passage, but reality offers only a freshly painted wall. It is clean, too clean, except under low light, where scraped plaque edges and a damaged ring mark are barely visible. The ring mark must keep the shape and scratched-removal texture of the fixed prop `Scratched Ring Mark.jpg`.

Nia thinks the old handbook route has reached a dead end and starts documenting it. Then the old handbook in Muci's arms begins to glow. It does not simply open; it lifts as if something behind the wall is pulling it, pages fluttering, and passes straight through the wall.

The three freeze. Muci quietly asks whether this counts as school property damage. Nia does not wait for him to finish. She touches the wall and finds that it lets her hand pass through like cold water. She goes first. Padarana checks that nobody outside is watching and follows. Muci goes last, wearing the expression of someone who knows this is definitely not a normal night tour.

Behind the wall is not an empty room but a sealed old corridor. The light is faint, the air smells like paper and old wood, and the wall language matches the old Student Store labels. The handbook stops deeper inside the corridor, waiting for them to continue.

This episode prioritizes suspense, spatial discovery, and pacing. The skincare theme remains lightly present through version verification and the idea that official summaries may not tell the whole story.

### Ending Hook
The old handbook stops at the end of the corridor and opens itself to a missing-page marker. Inside the wall, lights flicker on one by one, revealing a barely readable door label: Observation Archive.

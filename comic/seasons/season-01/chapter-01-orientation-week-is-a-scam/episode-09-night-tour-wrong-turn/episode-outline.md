# Episode 9 - Night Tour, Wrong Turn

## Type
Night campus suspense / sneaking episode / pre-cliffhanger

## Main characters
- Muci
- Padaruna
- Snacri
- Artrans

## Episode promise
During a school-sanctioned night tour, curiosity finally pushes the group into a physically hidden part of campus, and the erased Floor 4 mystery becomes a real place instead of a rumor.

## Core plot
The school organizes a night tour, expecting first-years to stay on the approved route. Muci deliberately falls behind so he can check one of the vanished spaces on the old map. Padaruna follows because she immediately senses trouble. Snacri appears with the unnerving energy of someone who expected this exact mistake.

The three reach a quiet old hallway whose walls have been repainted but not entirely erased. Old signage shadows still linger near a sealed frame, including traces of “4F Access.” Before they can examine it properly, Artrans appears. He sounds lazy, but for the first time his tension is obvious.

This episode is where the chapter drops almost all pretense of normal campus life.

## Skincare knowledge
- No major teaching beat here; the priority is suspense, architecture, and the emotional shift.

## American humor beats
- Artrans classifies the trio as brave, lost, or offensively bad at following directions.
- Padaruna's ability to get into danger remains weirdly enthusiastic.

## Foreshadowing
- Floor 4 is an active cover-up, not a forgotten rumor.
- Artrans knows the hidden spaces too well to be an outsider.

## Ending hook
The group ducks into an old reading room, and Muci accidentally knocks over a stack of aging files.

# Episode 9 - Night Tour, Wrong Turn

## 中文

### 类型
夜间校园悬疑 / 偷偷调查 / Chapter cliffhanger 前奏

### 主要角色
- Muci
- Padaruna
- Snacri
- Artrans

### Episode 承诺
学校安排的 night tour 本该是安全、漂亮、被控制的校园体验，但 Muci 小组利用旧地图和书签提示偏离路线。Floor 4 从传言变成真实存在过的空间。

### 核心剧情
夜游开始时，Sunny Spritz 仍旧把路线讲得像精心包装过的校园广告。Muci 注意到旧地图上有一段路线在新版导览中被刻意绕开，书签上的 “where the map refuses to go” 正好指向那里。

Padaruna 立刻跟上，因为她无法拒绝漂亮的危险。Snacri 像早就知道他们会走错一样出现。三人抵达一条安静的旧 hallway，墙面被重新粉刷过，但旧标识的影子还残留着，尤其是 “4F Access” 的痕迹。

Artrans 突然出现，语气仍然懒散，却第一次露出紧张。他没有解释所有事情，只警告他们：有些被删掉的路线不是为了让学生迷路，而是为了让某些人找不到入口。

### 护肤知识
本话不承担主要教学功能，重点放在悬疑、建筑空间和章节情绪转折。

### 连续性锁定
- old restricted hallway、旧地图、4F Access 残影必须和前后 prompt 保持连续。
- 如果夜游路线牌或旧门框反复出现，需要先做物件/场景统一参考。

### 美式喜剧点
- Artrans 把他们分类为勇敢、迷路，或者“对指示牌缺乏基本尊重”。
- Padaruna 对危险的热情让 Muci 更害怕。
- Snacri 像一个低电量但精准的 GPS。

### 伏笔
- Floor 4 是主动遮蔽，不是校园传说。
- Artrans 对隐藏空间过于熟悉。

### 结尾钩子
一阵脚步声逼近，小组躲进旧 reading room，Muci 不小心撞倒一叠泛黄文件。

## English

### Type
Night campus suspense / covert investigation / pre-cliffhanger

### Main Characters
- Muci
- Padaruna
- Snacri
- Artrans

### Episode Promise
The school night tour should be safe, polished, and controlled, but Muci's group uses the old map and bookmark clue to leave the route. Floor 4 stops being a rumor and becomes a space that physically existed.

### Core Plot
At the start of the night tour, Sunny Spritz presents the route like a carefully packaged campus advertisement. Muci notices that the old map includes a path the new tour deliberately avoids, and the bookmark line “where the map refuses to go” points there.

Padaruna follows immediately because she cannot resist beautiful danger. Snacri appears with the energy of someone who knew they would go the wrong way. The three reach a quiet old hallway whose walls have been repainted, but shadows of old signage remain, especially traces of “4F Access.”

Artrans appears suddenly. His voice is still lazy, but for the first time he looks tense. He does not explain everything; he only warns them that some deleted routes are not meant to keep students from getting lost, but to keep certain people from finding the entrance.

### Skincare Knowledge
No major teaching beat here; the priority is suspense, architecture, and the emotional turn of the chapter.

### Continuity Locks
- The old restricted hallway, old map, and 4F Access signage traces must stay continuous across prompts.
- If night-tour signs or old door frames recur, create unified prop or scene references first.

### American Humor Beats
- Artrans classifies them as brave, lost, or “fundamentally disrespectful to signage.”
- Padaruna's enthusiasm for danger makes Muci more scared.
- Snacri functions like a low-battery but accurate GPS.

### Foreshadowing
- Floor 4 is an active concealment, not a campus rumor.
- Artrans knows the hidden spaces too well.

### Ending Hook
Footsteps approach, the group ducks into an old reading room, and Muci accidentally knocks over a stack of yellowed files.

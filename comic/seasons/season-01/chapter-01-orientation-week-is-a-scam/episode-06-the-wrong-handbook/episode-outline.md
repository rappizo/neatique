# Episode 6 - The Wrong Handbook

## 中文

### 类型
轻推理 / Snacri 角色登场 / 小组化学反应

### 主要角色
- Muci
- Snacri
- Nia
- Padaruna
- Padarana

### Episode 承诺
Muci 终于开始把 handbook 的异常当成问题。Snacri 在本话正式登场，用 “Save Everyone by Doing Less” 的介绍框建立她少说、少动、却能直接看见重点的侦查气质。

### 核心剧情
Muci 把旧 handbook、Sunscreen Field 版本、宿舍 checklist 和课堂材料摊在一起，试图找出到底哪里不一致。Padaruna 把这当成校园阴谋板游戏，Nia 则无法忍受文件系统不一致，Padarana 负责让讨论不要过热。

Snacri 第一次出现时几乎没有戏剧动作。她只是看了一眼，就指出 Muci 做错了方向：不用比较所有内容，只要看删掉了什么。她把新版文件中消失的词圈出来：observation、Floor 4、archives、individual variation。

本话是角色互动的第一轮成型。Padaruna 提供动能，Nia 提供结构，Padarana 提供稳定，Snacri 提供切口，Muci 成为把大家连接在一起的人。

### 护肤知识
- 长期观察比一次性结论更可靠。
- Skin log 的意义是追踪模式，而不是每天给自己打分。
- 少做一点、看清一点，有时比立刻修正更有效。

### 视觉与角色锁定
- Snacri 第一次正式登场时使用介绍框：Snacri - Save Everyone by Doing Less。
- Snacri 的喜剧点来自低能耗、高命中率。她不需要夸张动作；她越安静，观察越准。
- Sunscreen Field Handbook 仍需保持 Episode 2 Page 10 已 approve 的设计。

### 美式喜剧点
- Padaruna 把证据整理成“美学犯罪墙”。
- Nia 被不一致文件逼到不得不加入调查。
- Snacri 用最少的句子让所有人的努力显得太吵。

### 伏笔
- Floor 4、archives 和 individual variation 被新版材料系统性删除。
- The Glow Thesis 可能不是单份文件，而是一套被拆散的旧理念。

### 结尾钩子
Snacri 从 Muci 的旧 handbook 夹层里抽出一张小纸条：Do not trust the current campus map.

## English

### Type
Light mystery / Snacri introduction / group chemistry

### Main Characters
- Muci
- Snacri
- Nia
- Padaruna
- Padarana

### Episode Promise
Muci finally starts treating the handbook mismatch as a real problem. Snacri formally enters here with the intro box “Save Everyone by Doing Less,” establishing her low-effort, high-accuracy investigative energy.

### Core Plot
Muci lays out the old handbook, the Sunscreen Field version, the dorm checklist, and the class materials, trying to understand where they conflict. Padaruna treats the process like a campus conspiracy board game, Nia cannot tolerate inconsistent documents, and Padarana keeps the discussion from overheating.

Snacri's first appearance is almost undramatic. She looks once and points out that Muci is comparing the wrong thing: they do not need every difference, only what has been removed. She circles the words missing from newer materials: observation, Floor 4, archives, individual variation.

This is the first episode where group chemistry takes shape. Padaruna brings momentum, Nia brings structure, Padarana brings steadiness, Snacri brings the angle of attack, and Muci becomes the person connecting them.

### Skincare Knowledge
- Long-term observation is more reliable than one-day conclusions.
- A skin log tracks patterns; it is not a daily self-grade.
- Doing less and seeing more clearly can be more useful than rushing to correct.

### Visual And Character Locks
- Use the first-appearance intro box: Snacri - Save Everyone by Doing Less.
- Snacri's comedy comes from low energy and high accuracy. She does not need big gestures; the quieter she is, the sharper the observation lands.
- The Sunscreen Field Handbook must continue matching the approved Episode 2 Page 10 design.

### American Humor Beats
- Padaruna turns evidence organization into an aesthetic crime wall.
- Nia is forced into the investigation by document inconsistency.
- Snacri uses the fewest possible sentences to make everyone else seem too loud.

### Foreshadowing
- Floor 4, archives, and individual variation have been systematically removed from newer materials.
- The Glow Thesis may not be one document, but a scattered older philosophy.

### Ending Hook
Snacri pulls a hidden slip from Muci's old handbook: Do not trust the current campus map.

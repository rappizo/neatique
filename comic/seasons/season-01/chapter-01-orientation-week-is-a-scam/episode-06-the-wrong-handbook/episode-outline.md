# Episode 6 - The Wrong Handbook

## Type
Light mystery / clue comparison / group formation

## Main characters
- Muci
- Snacri
- Padaruna
- Nia

## Episode promise
Artrans's warning pushes Muci into a real investigation, and the freshman handbook turns from a campus oddity into the first piece of evidence that school history has been edited.

## Core plot
Muci can no longer treat the handbook as a funny printing mistake, so he starts comparing his version to newer ones. Snacri joins immediately because she already noticed inconsistencies. Padaruna first treats the whole thing like aesthetic campus conspiracy content, but slowly starts to realize it is not just vibes.

Nia resists getting involved until the contradictions become too obvious to ignore. Between the different copies, they notice missing floor maps, replaced slogans, erased references to archives, and the complete disappearance of Floor 4 from newer editions.

The episode is the moment the group stops passively reacting and starts choosing to investigate.

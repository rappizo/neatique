# Episode 2 - Sunscreen Drill

## 中文

### 类型
校园训练喜剧 / Coach Ray 角色登场 / 防晒知识入门

### 主要角色
- Muci
- Coach Ray
- Sunny Spritz
- background first-year students

### Episode 承诺
Muci 以为自己只是要听一场普通防晒说明，结果被带进 Sunscreen Field 的超规格训练。Coach Ray 在本话正式登场，并用“Make the Sun Respect Boundaries”的介绍框确立他像盾牌一样保护边界的荒诞英雄感。

### 核心剧情
Sunny Spritz 把新生带到 Sunscreen Field，宣布这只是一个“轻松的防晒说明”。下一秒，Coach Ray 像训练营教官一样登场，把云层、反射光、补涂时机和户外等待全部当成战术问题。

本话不要出现 Padaruna、Nia、Snacri。重点只放在 Muci 和 Coach Ray 的节奏差：Muci 想听普通讲座，Coach Ray 却把防晒讲成边界守护训练。Muci 在一连串 cloud-cover simulation、reapplication drill 和快速问答中努力保持礼貌，同时越来越怀疑这所学校是不是把护肤常识发展成了校园运动。

训练结束时，Muci 发现自己的 Sunscreen Field Handbook 和现场投影版本不完全一致。封面与内页看似只是旧版，但某些句子更接近“observation”而不是“discipline”。这个小差异把本话从纯喜剧轻轻推向谜团。

### 护肤知识
- 防晒习惯在阴天和等待时间里也重要。
- 预防最好变成日常流程，而不是晒伤后的紧急补救。
- 补涂、暴露时间和反射环境都比“今天有没有大太阳”更可靠。

### 美式喜剧点
- Coach Ray 把云当成学术敌人。
- Muci 试图用正常课堂礼貌回应不正常的操场训练。
- Sunny Spritz 坚称这很轻松，现场却像防晒版本的体能测试。

### 伏笔
- Muci 的 handbook 版本和现场标准版本不一致。
- 旧版语言第一次暗示学校过去更重视观察，而不是统一纪律。

### 结尾钩子
Muci 把自己的 handbook 和训练场投影对照，意识到差异不是印刷错误那么简单。

## English

### Type
Campus training comedy / Coach Ray introduction / sunscreen knowledge primer

### Main Characters
- Muci
- Coach Ray
- Sunny Spritz
- background first-year students

### Episode Promise
Muci expects a normal sunscreen lecture and is pulled into an overproduced Sunscreen Field drill instead. Coach Ray formally enters here, with the intro box “Make the Sun Respect Boundaries,” establishing his shield-like boundary-protector energy.

### Core Plot
Sunny Spritz brings the first-years to Sunscreen Field and announces a “relaxed sunscreen briefing.” Coach Ray appears one beat later like a training-camp instructor, treating cloud cover, reflected light, reapplication timing, and outdoor waiting as tactical problems.

Padaruna, Nia, and Snacri must not appear in this episode. The focus is the rhythm clash between Muci and Coach Ray: Muci expects a lecture, while Coach Ray turns sunscreen into boundary defense training. Muci survives cloud-cover simulations, reapplication drills, and rapid-fire questions while privately wondering whether the school has turned basic skincare into a campus sport.

At the end of the drill, Muci notices that his Sunscreen Field Handbook does not fully match the projected official version. The cover and pages seem like an older copy, but some wording leans toward “observation” rather than “discipline.” That small mismatch nudges the episode from pure comedy toward mystery.

### Skincare Knowledge
- Sunscreen habits matter on cloudy days and during outdoor waiting time.
- Prevention works best as routine, not emergency repair after sun damage.
- Reapplication, exposure time, and reflective surroundings matter more than whether the day looks dramatically sunny.

- Use the first-appearance intro box: Coach Ray - Make the Sun Respect Boundaries.

### American Humor Beats
- Coach Ray treats clouds like an academic enemy.
- Muci tries to respond with normal classroom politeness to very abnormal field training.
- Sunny Spritz insists this is casual while the scene plays like a sunscreen fitness test.

### Foreshadowing
- Muci's handbook version does not match the official field version.
- The older wording hints for the first time that the school once valued observation more than uniform discipline.

### Ending Hook
Muci compares his handbook against the field projection and realizes the difference may not be a printing error.

# Chapter 1 - Orientation Week Is a Scam

## 中文

### Chapter 目标
Chapter 1 负责完成读者入学：先让 Neatique Skincare College 看起来可爱、夸张、好笑，再慢慢露出校园历史被改写过的疑点。

这一版结构以“先认识人，再推进谜团”为核心。Episode 01 已经生成完成，剧情不再重做；Episode 02-04 每话集中推出一个关键角色，并在角色第一次正式登场时使用角色介绍框；Episode 05-06 让 2-3 个角色之间产生互动和化学反应；Episode 07 开始正式推进 The Glow Thesis 相关主线。

### 角色首次登场介绍框
- Coach Ray - Make the Sun Respect Boundaries
- Nia - Be Right About Everything
- Padaruna - Try Every Bad Idea Beautifully
- Snacri - Save Everyone by Doing Less
- Padarana - Keep the World Soft

介绍框只在该角色第一次正式登场时出现，之后不再重复。

### Episode 节奏
- Episode 01 - Welcome to Neatique: 保留已完成版本。Muci 入学，校园既童话又荒唐，最后注意到手册里一句不合时宜的旧话。
- Episode 02 - Sunscreen Drill: Coach Ray 单独登场。不要让 Padaruna、Nia、Snacri 在这一话出现；重点是 Muci 被带进 Sunscreen Field 的防晒训练荒诞感。
- Episode 03 - Dorm Shelf Inspection: Nia 单独登场。用宿舍架检查展示她“必须正确”的气质，也让 Muci 看到校园规则的细密程度。
- Episode 04 - Know Your Barrier or Go Home: Padaruna 单独登场。她把第一堂课当成可以漂亮犯错的挑战，和 Muci 的谨慎形成对照。
- Episode 05 - Hydration Greenhouse: Padarana 登场，并与 Muci 形成温柔、低速、观察型互动，让章节从纯喜剧缓一下。
- Episode 06 - The Wrong Handbook: Snacri 登场，开始把手册版本差异从笑点变成线索。她的“少做一点”变成侦查优势。
- Episode 07 - The Stamp Hunt That Got Weird: 从这里开始正式推进剧情。Stamp Hunt 变成对旧地图、旧铭牌和 Founder Walk 的调查。
- Episode 08 - The Student Store Problem: 校园商店把旧标语和旧物件重新带出来，暗示历史不是自然消失，而是被管理过。
- Episode 09 - Night Tour, Wrong Turn: 夜游让旧地图上的隐藏空间变成真实地点，Floor 4 不再只是传言。
- Episode 10 - The Name on the Page: The Glow Thesis 第一次以实物碎片出现，结尾引出 The Luster Circle。

### Chapter 悬念
到 Chapter 1 结尾，读者需要清楚三件事：Neatique 的旧理念更接近“观察与个体差异”，现代校园更强调“纪律与标准答案”；校园材料正在被悄悄替换；Muci 和新朋友们不是唯一在找 The Glow Thesis 的人。

## English

### Chapter Objective
Chapter 1 onboards the reader into Neatique Skincare College: the campus should feel lovable, excessive, and funny first, then gradually reveal that its history has been edited.

This version is organized around “meet the people first, then move the mystery.” Episode 01 is already produced and should not be rebuilt. Episodes 02-04 each spotlight one key character introduction with a first-appearance intro box. Episodes 05-06 focus on 2-3 character interactions. Episode 07 begins the direct plot push toward The Glow Thesis.

### First-Appearance Intro Boxes
- Coach Ray - Make the Sun Respect Boundaries
- Nia - Be Right About Everything
- Padaruna - Try Every Bad Idea Beautifully
- Snacri - Save Everyone by Doing Less
- Padarana - Keep the World Soft

Use each intro box only on that character's first formal appearance. Do not repeat it later.

### Episode Rhythm
- Episode 01 - Welcome to Neatique: Keep the completed version. Muci arrives, the campus feels fairytale-strange and absurd, and he notices an out-of-place old sentence in his handbook.
- Episode 02 - Sunscreen Drill: Coach Ray gets the solo introduction. Padaruna, Nia, and Snacri must not appear in this episode; the focus is Muci being dragged into the absurd seriousness of Sunscreen Field.
- Episode 03 - Dorm Shelf Inspection: Nia gets the solo introduction. A dorm shelf inspection shows her need to be correct and lets Muci see how detailed campus rules can become.
- Episode 04 - Know Your Barrier or Go Home: Padaruna gets the solo introduction. She treats the first class like a chance to fail beautifully, contrasting with Muci's caution.
- Episode 05 - Hydration Greenhouse: Padarana enters through a gentle, slower interaction with Muci, letting the chapter breathe after the early comedy.
- Episode 06 - The Wrong Handbook: Snacri enters and turns handbook version differences from a joke into evidence. Her instinct to do less becomes an investigative advantage.
- Episode 07 - The Stamp Hunt That Got Weird: The plot begins moving directly. The Stamp Hunt becomes an investigation into old maps, replaced plaques, and Founder Walk.
- Episode 08 - The Student Store Problem: The campus store brings old slogans and old objects back into view, suggesting history has been managed rather than naturally forgotten.
- Episode 09 - Night Tour, Wrong Turn: The night tour turns a hidden space from the old map into a physical location. Floor 4 is no longer just a rumor.
- Episode 10 - The Name on the Page: The first physical fragment of The Glow Thesis appears, and the ending introduces The Luster Circle.

### Chapter Payoff
By the end of Chapter 1, readers should understand three things: Neatique's older philosophy was closer to observation and individual difference, while the modern campus pushes discipline and standard answers; campus materials are being quietly replaced; and Muci's group is not the only one searching for The Glow Thesis.

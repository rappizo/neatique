# Chapter 1 - Orientation Week Is a Scam

## 中文

### Chapter 目标
Chapter 1 负责完成读者入学：先让 Neatique Skincare College 看起来可爱、夸张、好笑，再慢慢露出校园历史被改写过的疑点。

这一版结构以“先认识人，再让三人小组完成一次白天+夜晚校园探查活动”为核心。Episode 01 已经生成完成，剧情不重做；Episode 02-04 每话集中推出一个关键角色，并在角色第一次正式登场时使用角色介绍框；Episode 05-06 让少量角色之间产生互动和化学反应；Episode 07-10 只让 Muci、Nia 和 Padarana 作为主动调查者推进主线，让事件链更紧密、更适合漫画页拆分。

### 角色首次登场介绍框
- Coach Ray - Make the Sun Respect Boundaries
- Nia - Be Right About Everything
- Padaruna - Try Every Bad Idea Beautifully
- Snacri - Save Everyone by Doing Less
- Padarana - Keep the World Soft

介绍框只在该角色第一次正式登场时出现，之后不再重复。后续章节不需要为了介绍角色而让太多人同时出现。

### Episode 节奏
- Episode 01 - Welcome to Neatique: 保留已完成版本。Muci 入学，校园既童话又荒唐，最后注意到手册里一句不合时宜的旧话。
- Episode 02 - Sunscreen Drill: Coach Ray 单独登场。不要让 Padaruna、Nia、Snacri 在这一话出现；重点是 Muci 被带进 Sunscreen Field 的防晒训练荒诞感。
- Episode 03 - Dorm Shelf Inspection: Nia 单独登场。用宿舍架检查展示她“必须正确”的气质，也让 Muci 看到校园规则的细密程度。
- Episode 04 - Know Your Barrier or Go Home: Padaruna 单独登场。她把第一堂课当成可以漂亮犯错的挑战，和 Muci 的谨慎形成对照。
- Episode 05 - Hydration Greenhouse: Padarana 登场，并与 Muci 形成温柔、低速、观察型互动，让章节从纯喜剧缓一下。
- Episode 06 - The Wrong Handbook: Snacri 登场，开始把手册版本差异从笑点变成线索。她的“少做一点”变成侦查优势，但后续 7-10 不需要她参与主线行动。
- Episode 07 - The Store List Does Not Match: Muci、Nia 和 Padarana 被分到同一个白天+夜晚校园探查活动小组。三人先去 Student Store 采购活动用品，发现商品标语、旧书签、货架说明和最新学生手册明显对不上。
- Episode 08 - The Day Map Says Otherwise: 白天探查开始。三人拿着旧地图、最新手册和旧铭牌痕迹对比 Founder Walk、Student Store 外侧、Hydration Greenhouse 周边等地点，发现很多改变不是自然翻新，而像是被系统性替换。
- Episode 09 - The Handbook Goes Through the Wall: 夜晚探查时，Nia 提议按 Old Version handbook 的路线再查一次。路线尽头只有一面普通墙。旧 handbook 突然从 Muci 身边发光飞出，穿过墙面；Nia 第一个跟着冲过去，也穿过墙，Muci 和 Padarana 被迫跟上。
- Episode 10 - The Fragment Behind the Wall: 三人在隐藏区域短暂探查，发现 The Glow Thesis 的残页。时间快到，他们必须赶回活动集合点报告，只能匆忙带着碎片离开。结尾把旧物件、环形印记和 The Luster Circle 的名字连起来。

### Chapter 悬念
到 Chapter 1 结尾，读者需要清楚三件事：Neatique 的旧理念更接近“观察与个体差异”，现代校园更强调“纪律与标准答案”；校园材料、商品话术和空间路线正在被悄悄替换；Muci、Nia 和 Padarana 已经拿到 The Glow Thesis 的第一片实物证据。

### 角色与对话密度
后半章必须保持 2-3 个主动角色。Episode 07-10 的核心事件都由 Muci、Nia 和 Padarana 参与即可，不要再把 Padaruna、Snacri、Artrans 或 Sunny Spritz 放进同一组主要对话。其他角色最多作为背景、广播、海报或被提及的存在出现。

### 场景包
使用 `scene-refs/` 中锁定的 Chapter 1 环境参考。Sunscreen Field、Dorm/Residence、Hydration Greenhouse、Founder Walk、Student Store、old restricted hallway/hidden area 等场景必须保持连续性。反复出现的手册、旧地图、铭牌、书签、The Glow Thesis 残页等物件要在生成 prompt 前先做统一参考。

## English

### Chapter Objective
Chapter 1 onboards the reader into Neatique Skincare College: the campus should feel lovable, excessive, and funny first, then gradually reveal that its history has been edited.

This version is organized around “meet the people first, then let a three-character team complete one day-and-night campus investigation.” Episode 01 is already produced and should not be rebuilt. Episodes 02-04 each spotlight one key character introduction with a first-appearance intro box. Episodes 05-06 focus on small character interactions. Episodes 07-10 keep the active investigation to Muci, Nia, and Padarana so the plot chain is tighter and easier to turn into clear comic pages.

### First-Appearance Intro Boxes
- Coach Ray - Make the Sun Respect Boundaries
- Nia - Be Right About Everything
- Padaruna - Try Every Bad Idea Beautifully
- Snacri - Save Everyone by Doing Less
- Padarana - Keep the World Soft

Use each intro box only on that character's first formal appearance. Do not repeat it later. Later chapters should not crowd many characters into one event just to keep everyone visible.

### Episode Rhythm
- Episode 01 - Welcome to Neatique: Keep the completed version. Muci arrives, the campus feels fairytale-strange and absurd, and he notices an out-of-place old sentence in his handbook.
- Episode 02 - Sunscreen Drill: Coach Ray gets the solo introduction. Padaruna, Nia, and Snacri must not appear in this episode; the focus is Muci being dragged into the absurd seriousness of Sunscreen Field.
- Episode 03 - Dorm Shelf Inspection: Nia gets the solo introduction. A dorm shelf inspection shows her need to be correct and lets Muci see how detailed campus rules can become.
- Episode 04 - Know Your Barrier or Go Home: Padaruna gets the solo introduction. She treats the first class like a chance to fail beautifully, contrasting with Muci's caution.
- Episode 05 - Hydration Greenhouse: Padarana enters through a gentle, slower interaction with Muci, letting the chapter breathe after the early comedy.
- Episode 06 - The Wrong Handbook: Snacri enters and turns handbook version differences from a joke into evidence. Her instinct to do less becomes an investigative advantage, but she does not need to join the main action in Episodes 07-10.
- Episode 07 - The Store List Does Not Match: Muci, Nia, and Padarana are assigned to the same day-and-night campus investigation group. They first visit the Student Store to buy activity supplies and notice that product slogans, old bookmarks, shelf language, and the latest student handbook contradict each other.
- Episode 08 - The Day Map Says Otherwise: The daytime investigation begins. The three compare the old map, the latest handbook, and old plaque traces around Founder Walk, the Student Store exterior, and the Hydration Greenhouse area, realizing the changes look systematic rather than naturally renovated.
- Episode 09 - The Handbook Goes Through the Wall: During the night investigation, Nia suggests checking the Old Version handbook route. The route ends at a plain wall. The old handbook suddenly glows near Muci and flies through the wall; Nia follows first and also passes through, forcing Muci and Padarana to follow.
- Episode 10 - The Fragment Behind the Wall: The three briefly explore the hidden area and discover a torn fragment of The Glow Thesis. Time is running out, so they must rush back to the activity meeting point with the fragment. The ending connects the old objects, the ring mark, and the name The Luster Circle.

### Chapter Payoff
By the end of Chapter 1, readers should understand three things: Neatique's older philosophy was closer to observation and individual difference, while the modern campus pushes discipline and standard answers; campus materials, merchandise language, and route design are being quietly replaced; and Muci, Nia, and Padarana now hold the first physical fragment of The Glow Thesis.

### Cast And Dialogue Density
Keep the back half of the chapter focused on 2-3 active characters. Episodes 07-10 should be driven by Muci, Nia, and Padarana only. Do not add Padaruna, Snacri, Artrans, or Sunny Spritz into the same main conversation group. Other characters can appear only as background presence, announcements, posters, or brief mentions.

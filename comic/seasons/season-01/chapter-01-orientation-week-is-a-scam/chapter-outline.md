# Chapter 1 - Orientation Week Is a Scam

## Chapter objective
Chapter 1 completes three core jobs:

- make readers fall in love with Neatique Skincare College as a whimsical but slightly suspicious campus world
- establish Muci as the audience surrogate and emotional point of entry
- introduce The Glow Thesis for the first time and hint that the school has rewritten its own history

## Episode beats
- Episodes 01-05 stay light, funny, and day-in-the-life focused so the campus feels lovable before the mystery takes over.
- Episodes 06-10 gradually tip the chapter into investigation, old handbooks, altered maps, and the first clear mention of The Glow Thesis.
- The chapter rhythm should move from orientation absurdity to institutional unease without losing warmth or comedy.

## Cliffhanger / payoff
By the end of Chapter 1, the cast should understand that:

- the school once used a different philosophy language
- old campus materials are being quietly replaced
- The Glow Thesis is real
- someone besides the protagonists may already be searching for it

# Episode 1 - Welcome to Neatique

## Type
Slice of life / move-in opening / campus comedy

## Main characters
- Muci
- Sunny Spritz
- background first-year students

## Episode promise
Muci arrives at Neatique Skincare College expecting an elegant skincare academy and instead gets swallowed by a wildly theatrical orientation system that immediately tells him this campus is beautiful, excessive, and slightly unhinged.

## Core plot
Muci reaches the gates of Neatique Skincare College on his first day and is instantly overwhelmed by the fairytale campus design, the huge freshman turnout, and Sunny Spritz's cheerleader-meets-crisis-manager orientation energy. Every check-in step feels ten percent too intense: the welcome line moves like airport security, the welcome kit is absurdly oversized, and the directional signs point to places that sound more like theme park attractions than academic buildings.

Muci is less dazzled than confused. He keeps trying to confirm that this is, in fact, still a college. The episode uses his normal-person reactions to make the campus lovable and funny while quietly teaching the reader that this world takes sunscreen, barrier care, and hydration seriously enough to build culture around them.

Near the end, Muci finds a strange line in his handbook that does not match the current school slogan: “Healthy skin starts with knowing what not to chase.” The sentence feels older, wiser, and slightly out of place.

## Skincare knowledge
- The school culture values barrier care, hydration, and sunscreen awareness.
- Foundational skincare is treated as normal student life rather than emergency repair.
- The episode only teases the knowledge layer without over-explaining it.

## American humor beats
- Orientation feels like military-grade hospitality.
- Sunny Spritz sounds like a cheer captain running a public-safety drill.
- Muci grounds the whole episode by asking the exact sane question nobody else is asking.

## Foreshadowing
- The handbook contains language that does not sound like the modern institution.
- The school may have changed its philosophy messaging over time.

## Ending hook
Muci stares at the handbook line and realizes it sounds nothing like the campus branding around him.

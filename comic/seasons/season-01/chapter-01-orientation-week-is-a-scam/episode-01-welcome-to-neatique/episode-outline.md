# Episode 1 - Welcome to Neatique

## 中文

### 类型
入学开场 / 校园喜剧 / 世界观建立

### 主要角色
- Muci
- Sunny Spritz
- background first-year students

### Episode 承诺
Muci 以为自己进入的是一所优雅的护肤学院，却被 Neatique Skincare College 过度热情、过度戏剧化、过度讲究流程的迎新系统吞没。读者通过他的正常人反应，第一次感受到这个校园既漂亮又荒唐。

### 核心剧情
Muci 抵达校门，立刻被童话式建筑、新生人潮和 Sunny Spritz 的高能迎新节奏包围。签到队伍像机场安检，welcome kit 大得离谱，指示牌上的地点名称又像主题乐园项目又像严肃学院设施。

Muci 不断确认“这里真的还是学校吗”。Episode 的喜剧来自他和校园文化之间的错位：所有人都把防晒、屏障护理、补水和日常流程当成严肃生活技能，只有 Muci 还在努力理解规则。

结尾时，Muci 在自己的 handbook 里看到一句和现代校园标语不一致的旧话：“Healthy skin starts with knowing what not to chase.” 这句话更温和、更老派，也明显不属于他周围那套热闹的品牌语言。

### 护肤知识
- 校园文化重视屏障护理、补水和防晒意识。
- 基础护肤被当成日常生活能力，而不是出了问题才补救。
- 本话只轻轻铺垫知识层，不做课堂式解释。

### 美式喜剧点
- 迎新像军规级 hospitality。
- Sunny Spritz 像啦啦队队长在指挥公共安全演习。
- Muci 问出了所有正常人都会问、但没人敢问的问题。

### 伏笔
- Handbook 里有不属于现代校园话术的旧句子。
- 学校可能曾经修改过自己的理念表达。

### 结尾钩子
Muci 盯着那句旧话，意识到它和整个校园现在展示出来的气质完全不同。

## English

### Type
Move-in opening / campus comedy / worldbuilding

### Main Characters
- Muci
- Sunny Spritz
- background first-year students

### Episode Promise
Muci expects an elegant skincare academy and instead gets swallowed by Neatique Skincare College's overly theatrical orientation system. Through his normal-person reactions, readers learn that the campus is beautiful, excessive, and slightly strange.

### Core Plot
Muci reaches the gates and is immediately surrounded by fairytale architecture, freshman crowds, and Sunny Spritz's high-energy orientation rhythm. The check-in line feels like airport security, the welcome kit is absurdly oversized, and the signs point toward places that sound halfway between theme-park attractions and serious academic buildings.

Muci keeps trying to confirm that this is, in fact, still a school. The comedy comes from his mismatch with campus culture: everyone treats sunscreen, barrier care, hydration, and routine discipline like normal life skills, while Muci is still trying to understand the rules.

Near the end, Muci finds an old line in his handbook that does not match the modern campus slogans: “Healthy skin starts with knowing what not to chase.” It feels gentler, older, and clearly out of step with the cheerful branding around him.

### Skincare Knowledge
- The campus values barrier care, hydration, and sunscreen awareness.
- Foundational skincare is treated as daily life, not emergency repair.
- The episode only hints at the knowledge layer instead of explaining it heavily.

### American Humor Beats
- Orientation feels like military-grade hospitality.
- Sunny Spritz sounds like a cheer captain running a public-safety drill.
- Muci asks the sane question everyone else has somehow skipped.

### Foreshadowing
- The handbook contains language that does not belong to the modern institution.
- The school may have changed how it talks about its own philosophy.

### Ending Hook
Muci stares at the old sentence and realizes it sounds nothing like the campus branding around him.

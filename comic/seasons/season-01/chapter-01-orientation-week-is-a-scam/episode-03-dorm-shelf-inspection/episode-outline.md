# Episode 3 - Dorm Shelf Inspection

## Type
Dorm comedy / character chemistry setup

## Main characters
- Muci
- Padaruna
- Snacri
- Padarana
- Mira Mistwell

## Episode promise
Muci finally reaches the dorm expecting rest, only to discover that even dorm life at Neatique is governed by strangely detailed skincare etiquette and quietly revealing personality clashes.

## Core plot
Muci enters the freshman dorm and thinks he can finally settle in, but the room is already a battlefield of routine philosophies. Padaruna has brought enough product to stock a boutique. Snacri has almost nothing on her shelf. Muci is stuck in the middle, unsure what counts as normal.

Their RA, Mira Mistwell, arrives to conduct a Dorm Shelf Inspection that sounds absurdly polite but feels weirdly official. She explains the shelf zones, sanitation rules, towel ventilation expectations, and even the concept of “emotional hoarding” at the sink.

Padarana makes her first gentle impression here by helping Muci navigate the tension without taking over the scene. The episode builds the emotional shape of the dorm while showing that even everyday routines at Neatique are social, performative, and regulated.

## Skincare knowledge
- Hygiene and storage shape how useful products actually remain.
- More products do not automatically mean a better routine.
- A routine should be sustainable, not clutter-driven.

## American humor beats
- Mira's calm professionalism makes the rules even funnier.
- Padaruna's shelf looks like an unlicensed retail launch.
- Muci is baffled that “routine noise” is apparently a dorm governance issue.

## Foreshadowing
- A torn old notice mentions that Floor 4 access remains restricted.
- Mira pauses just a little too long before dismissing the question.

## Ending hook
When Muci asks what happened on Floor 4, Mira gives a polite non-answer that feels more suspicious than comforting.

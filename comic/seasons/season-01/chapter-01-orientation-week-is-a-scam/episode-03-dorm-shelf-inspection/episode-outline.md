# Episode 3 - Dorm Shelf Inspection

## 中文

### 类型
宿舍喜剧 / Nia 角色登场 / 规则型人格展示

### 主要角色
- Muci
- Nia
- Mira Mistwell
- background dorm students

### Episode 承诺
Muci 终于到达宿舍，以为自己可以休息，却遇到 Neatique 对宿舍置物、卫生和 routine 展示的细密要求。Nia 在本话第一次正式登场，并用 “Be Right About Everything” 的介绍框建立她必须准确、必须正确的气质。

### 核心剧情
Muci 进入新生宿舍后，还没来得及放下行李，就遇到 Mira Mistwell 的 Dorm Shelf Inspection。检查内容听起来礼貌，实际却夸张到包括产品分区、瓶口朝向、毛巾通风、过期标记和“routine noise”。

Nia 的架子像标准答案本身：物品少、顺序清楚、标签准确，几乎让 Mira 都显得多余。Muci 的东西则被欢迎套装和旧 handbook 挤得乱七八糟。Nia 并不刻薄，但她每一句纠正都准确得让人无处可躲。

检查中，Muci 发现自己的旧 handbook 里有一条宿舍规则和 Mira 手里的新版 checklist 不一致。Nia 起初把它当作 Muci 没读清楚，但她自己核对后也短暂停顿。

### 护肤知识
- 卫生、储存和使用顺序会影响产品是否真的有效。
- 产品多不等于 routine 好。
- 可持续的 routine 比看起来丰富的 routine 更重要。

### 视觉与角色锁定
- Nia 第一次正式登场时使用介绍框：Nia - Be Right About Everything。
- Nia 的表现要精准、冷静、答题式，不要把她画成普通高冷角色；她的喜剧点来自“正确到过分”。

### 美式喜剧点
- Dorm Shelf Inspection 像把宿舍生活变成合规审计。
- Muci 发现自己连毛巾都能摆错。
- Nia 的每次帮助都像在温柔地发布判决书。

### 伏笔
- 新旧宿舍规则第一次出现具体冲突。
- Floor 4 的旧访问提示被新版 checklist 删除。

### 结尾钩子
Nia 合上 checklist，说这不是 Muci 看错了，而是两份文件确实不一样。

## English

### Type
Dorm comedy / Nia introduction / rule-minded character showcase

### Main Characters
- Muci
- Nia
- Mira Mistwell
- background dorm students

### Episode Promise
Muci finally reaches the dorm expecting rest, only to find that Neatique has detailed expectations for storage, hygiene, and routine display. Nia formally appears here with the intro box “Be Right About Everything,” establishing her need for precision and correctness.

### Core Plot
Muci enters the freshman dorm and barely sets down his bags before Mira Mistwell begins a Dorm Shelf Inspection. The inspection sounds polite but covers product zones, bottle direction, towel ventilation, expiration marks, and “routine noise.”

Nia's shelf looks like the answer key itself: minimal, orderly, accurately labeled, and so correct that Mira almost seems unnecessary. Muci's shelf is a mess of welcome-kit items and his older handbook. Nia is not cruel, but every correction she offers is so accurate that Muci has nowhere to hide.

During the inspection, Muci notices that one dorm rule in his old handbook does not match Mira's newer checklist. Nia first assumes he misread it, then checks for herself and briefly pauses.

### Skincare Knowledge
- Hygiene, storage, and sequence affect whether products stay useful.
- More products do not automatically make a better routine.
- A sustainable routine matters more than a visually impressive one.

### Visual And Character Locks
- Use the first-appearance intro box: Nia - Be Right About Everything.
- Nia should feel precise, calm, and answer-key accurate. Do not make her generic cold; the comedy comes from being almost impossibly correct.

### American Humor Beats
- Dorm Shelf Inspection turns dorm life into a compliance audit.
- Muci discovers that even his towel can be wrong.
- Nia's helpful corrections land like gentle legal rulings.

### Foreshadowing
- The old and new dorm rules show a concrete mismatch.
- An old Floor 4 access note has been removed from the newer checklist.

### Ending Hook
Nia closes the checklist and admits Muci did not misread it: the two documents really are different.

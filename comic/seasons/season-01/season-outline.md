# Season 1 - The Welcome Glow

## Season objective
Introduce Neatique Skincare College, form the core cast, and launch the mystery around The Glow Thesis.

## Emotional arc
From wonder and chaos to fragile alliance and real danger.

## Chapter flow
- **Chapter 1 - Orientation Week Is a Scam**: Muci enters Neatique Skincare College and discovers that the school is both polished and absurd. During orientation chaos, he accidentally finds the first fragment mentioning The Glow Thesis.
- **Chapter 2 - The Floor 4 Curfew**: Muci, Padaruna, and Snacri chase the archive clue into a sealed dorm floor and meet Artrans as a real participant in the mystery instead of a random eccentric upperclassman.
- **Chapter 3 - Nia Does Not Join Groups**: Nia catches manipulated lab data and reluctantly joins the group once she realizes the same hidden system may be observing students from inside the institution.
- **Chapter 4 - The Sheet Mask Heist**: A campus rush for archival masks turns into comedy and code-breaking when the packaging reveals another hidden trail tied to founder-era materials.
- **Chapter 5 - The Founder Didn't Lose It**: The group finds the first complete fragment and learns that the founder wrote, "There is no universal glow." The Luster Circle steps onto the page as an organized rival force.

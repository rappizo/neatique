# Episode 1 - Routine Wars

## Type
Dorm comedy / relationship-building / morning chaos

## Main characters
- Muci
- Padaruna
- Snacri
- Padarana
- Mira Mistwell

## Episode promise
The first truly normal dorm morning immediately proves there is no such thing as a normal routine at Neatique, and the cast's skincare habits become a funny but meaningful map of how they approach life.

## Core plot
The dorm finally settles into weekday rhythm, which only reveals new problems. Padaruna wants to try the latest campus-trending routine. Snacri keeps everything minimal. Muci is overwhelmed by the fact that every method seems justified by someone. Padarana becomes the soft voice reminding everyone that different skin states do not need the same answer.

Mira Mistwell reappears to inspect the common sink area and finds that Padaruna has already annexed too much shared counter space. The episode is funny and domestic, but it also grounds the group as actual roommates and friends before the next mystery push.

## Skincare knowledge
- A routine should fit the person using it.
- Simple routines are not laziness.
- Complicated routines are not automatically advanced or effective.

## American humor beats
- Mira calls out sink territorialism with the language of housing law.
- Muci watches ordinary morning care turn into ideological warfare.

## Foreshadowing
- Mira mentions that Floor 4 once used a personal routine log system.
- The wording connects back to the older handbook clues.

## Ending hook
Muci instantly connects “routine log” to the archive language from Chapter 1.

## Episode promise
A simple dorm curfew notice becomes the gateway to the next real clue, proving that Floor 4 was tied to an archive transfer and that even official bulletin boards are part of the cover-up.

## Core plot
The students notice a fresh curfew notice on the dorm board, but Muci realizes it has been placed over an older posting. When the group peels the corner back, they uncover text stating that Floor 4 access remained restricted until an archive transfer was complete.

The phrase instantly links back to The Glow Thesis and the Personal Glow Log Archive. Mira catches them looking, but instead of forcefully shutting them down, she delivers a warning that sounds protective and ominous at the same time.

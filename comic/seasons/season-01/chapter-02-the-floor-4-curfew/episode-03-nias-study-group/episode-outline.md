# Episode 3 - Nia's Study Group

## Type
Academic comedy / interpersonal friction / character deepening

## Main characters
- Muci
- Nia
- Snacri
- Padaruna
- Padarana

## Episode promise
Nia's attempt to create the perfect study group becomes a lesson in how useful structure can slowly turn into pressure when it stops leaving room for nuance.

## Core plot
Nia organizes a prep group for the first Barrier Sciences quiz and arrives armed with immaculate charts, category logic, and perfectly ordered explanations. Muci asks the most basic questions in the room, which initially frustrates her. Padaruna tries to participate with full enthusiasm but no respect for table formatting. Snacri points out that Nia's system is accurate but starting to sound like a single right answer.

Padarana acts as social glue, helping the group stay together while Nia slowly realizes that clarity can become overreach if it flattens complexity.

## Skincare knowledge
- Structure and knowledge are helpful, but skin response is not a fixed equation.
- Correct information still needs context.
- Logging patterns should support understanding, not ranking.

## American humor beats
- Nia's guide radiates bureaucratic excellence.
- Padaruna compares it to a tax form and somehow becomes more correct the longer she talks.

## Foreshadowing
- Nia's love of order makes her susceptible to institutional certainty.
- That trait will make her visible to the wrong people.

## Ending hook
Nia receives an anonymous invitation addressed to students who value clarity.

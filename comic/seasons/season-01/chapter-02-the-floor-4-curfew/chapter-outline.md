# Chapter 2 - The Floor 4 Curfew

## Chapter objective
Chapter 2 does two things at once:

- deepen the dorm-life, friendship, and campus-routine side of the world during the first half
- turn Floor 4 into the first real secret investigation during the second half

By the end of the chapter, readers should know the core group is not the only one searching for The Glow Thesis.

## Episode beats
- Episodes 01-05 build routine conflicts, learning pressure, trend temptation, and emotional fatigue inside everyday campus life.
- Episodes 06-10 pivot into curfew notices, deleted stairwells, Personal Glow Logs, and the realization that someone else has been investigating Floor 4.
- The chapter should feel like the moment the group stops being curious students and starts becoming a real mystery team.

## Cliffhanger / payoff
By the end of Chapter 2:

- Floor 4 should feel like a real hidden archive space, not just a rumor
- Personal Glow Logs should reveal a gentler older philosophy of skincare observation
- Nia should be visibly on the radar of the people behind the larger conspiracy
- readers should understand that The Luster Circle is not hunting pages alone, but trying to control interpretation

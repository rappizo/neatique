# Episode 7 - The Stairwell That Shouldn't Exist

## Type
Architectural mystery / hidden-space discovery

## Main characters
- Muci
- Snacri
- Padaruna
- Nia

## Episode promise
The group finally proves that the campus map is lying by locating a sealed stairwell that physically exists even though the official school layout pretends it does not.

## Core plot
Using the old handbook and new campus diagrams, the group compares building logic and discovers a structural inconsistency in the dorm. Nia initially resists illegal exploration, but the broken architecture is too mathematically offensive for her to ignore.

Together they find a section of wall that has been painted over to hide an old door. The scene moves from comedy into real investigative suspense as the erased version of campus space becomes undeniable.

## Skincare knowledge
- Nia explicitly links bad maps to bad judgment, echoing the way bad information creates bad routines.

## American humor beats
- Nia insists this is not trespassing, only architectural verification.
- Padaruna treats illegal discovery with the energy of a field trip.

## Foreshadowing
- The old door bears the same ring-like scratch mark seen elsewhere.
- Someone has hidden the route carefully, not casually.

## Ending hook
From behind the sealed entry, they hear the faint sound of paper moving.

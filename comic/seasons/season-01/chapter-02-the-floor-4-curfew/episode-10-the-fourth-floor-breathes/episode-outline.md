# Episode 10 - The Fourth Floor Breathes

## Type
Chapter climax / escape sequence / ideological reveal

## Main characters
- Muci
- Padaruna
- Nia
- Snacri
- Padarana
- Artrans

## Episode promise
An old warning system traps the cast inside Floor 4 long enough for Artrans to reveal what the real threat is: not just missing pages, but people trying to weaponize interpretation itself.

## Core plot
Without warning, the dormant Floor 4 warning system lights up. The signal does not feel like a modern alarm so much as an older manual alert. The group is forced deeper into the abandoned corridor network until Artrans appears and guides them out through the old reading room passage.

Muci demands to know why Artrans is always near the secret spaces. Artrans does not answer cleanly, but he finally gives the most important truth yet: the people hunting The Glow Thesis are not merely collecting pages. They are trying to find enough evidence to reinterpret it as a standard answer for everyone.

He then looks at Nia in a way that makes it clear she has already attracted the wrong attention.

# Episode 5 - Quiet Skin, Loud Campus

## Type
Emotional pause / state-awareness episode / friendship deepening

## Main characters
- Muci
- Padarana
- Snacri
- Padaruna

## Episode promise
As orientation exhaustion catches up with everyone, Padarana becomes the episode's emotional center by naming the pressure the others are still trying to outrun.

## Core plot
Several days of campus activity, rules, classes, and social noise start wearing the freshmen down. Muci's skin and mood both feel off. Padaruna still wants to say yes to everything. Nia is buried in academic structure. Snacri withdraws further into silence.

Padarana notices that the issue is not laziness or failure, but information overload and emotional strain. She brings Muci and Snacri to a quieter campus corner where the group can finally stop performing progress and simply observe how they feel.

This episode matters because it defines Padarana's role: she can hear the stress that other people are still translating into tasks and routines.

## Skincare knowledge
- Stress, sleep, and pace influence visible skin state.
- Rest is part of a routine, not the opposite of one.
- Skincare should not become another system of self-punishment.

## American humor beats
- Muci feels emotionally audited by his own face.
- The campus is so loud that silence itself feels like a luxury resource.

## Foreshadowing
- Padarana overhears upperclassmen mention that Floor 4 is being checked again.
- A light appears briefly in the distant dorm window.

## Ending hook
Padarana watches a fourth-floor window flare for just a second and realizes something is active there.

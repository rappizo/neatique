- Nia

## Episode promise
The group realizes the hidden archive is an active battleground, not a dead secret, and that another investigator is already ahead of them.

## Core plot
The team keeps searching the hidden Floor 4 area and quickly notices that some logs are missing while others show fresh disturbances. Snacri determines that these are recent traces, not old decay. They hear movement down the corridor and chase it, but the figure escapes before fully revealing themself.

The only real clue is an elegant silhouette and a glow-ring accessory that strongly suggests Vela Sheen without confirming it outright. Meanwhile, Nia finds that the removed records all share the same key idea: “standardization response.”

This is the point where the conspiracy stops being abstract and becomes a live competition over meaning.

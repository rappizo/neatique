# Season 2 - The Fast Results Trap

## Season objective
Turn the mystery into a competition by introducing seductive systems, social pressure, and elite recruitment.

## Emotional arc
From curiosity to temptation, pressure, and fracture risk.

## Chapter flow
- **Chapter 6 - Trending by Monday**: A campus obsession with a three-day glow routine causes widespread backlash and proves that trend velocity can be weaponized.
- **Chapter 7 - The Luster Fellowship**: An elite student program begins recruiting standout students, and Nia becomes a natural target for the polished logic of the Luster system.
- **Chapter 8 - Barrier Rehab Detention**: What starts as punishment becomes discovery when the group finds founder-era repair notes suggesting the manuscript may be a warning against excess, not a manual for power.
- **Chapter 9 - Snacri Has a Theory**: Snacri reframes the mystery by suggesting that the thesis was split apart to prevent misuse, not because it was lost.
- **Chapter 10 - The Dean Who Smiles Too Much**: Lucent Vale appears publicly and immediately changes the emotional pressure of the story, especially for Artrans.

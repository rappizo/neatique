# Season 3 - The Archived Lies

## Season objective
Reveal the school's falsified history and expose the ideological roots of the conflict.

## Emotional arc
From suspicion to betrayal, grief, and philosophical reversal.

## Chapter flow
- **Chapter 11 - Artrans Was There**: Artrans admits direct contact with the founder-era system and shifts from mysterious helper to implicated witness.
- **Chapter 12 - The Missing Archive Room**: The group finds proof that a whole layer of school history was deliberately erased during the same era in which the thesis was dismantled.
- **Chapter 13 - Padarana Hears What People Don't Say**: Padarana pieces together old tensions through tone, gossip, and side-channel memory, revealing that the Luster Circle grew from a founder-era ideological split.
- **Chapter 14 - The Prototype**: A hidden experiment record reveals Glow Standard Prototype, showing that Lucent's logic has been institutional for years, not recently invented.
- **Chapter 15 - There Was Never a Formula**: The cast learns that The Glow Thesis is not a perfect-formula book at all, but a record arguing against standard answers in skincare and self-definition.

# Season 4 - Who Gets to Define Glow?

## Season objective
Bring the war into public campus life and force the cast to fight over meaning, standards, and institutional power.

## Emotional arc
From resistance to fracture, confrontation, and interpretive victory.

## Chapter flow
- **Chapter 16 - The Glow Index**: Lucent Vale launches a campus scoring system that turns skin into rankable status and makes the school visibly colder.
- **Chapter 17 - Muci Refuses to Rank People**: Muci publicly rejects the Glow Index and helps transform the group from quiet investigators into open opposition.
- **Chapter 18 - The Break-In at Founder Hall**: The cast breaks into Founder Hall for the final core text and is finally forced into direct confrontation with Lucent Vale.
- **Chapter 19 - Lucent Vale Is Not Wrong Enough**: Lucent makes his strongest case: people crave certainty, not freedom. The cast must answer a logic that feels useful, not cartoonishly evil.
- **Chapter 20 - The Thesis of Your Own Skin**: The cast publishes the true meaning of the manuscript as an anti-standard manifesto and graduates with understanding instead of a single best answer.

# Mira Mistwell

## Chinese name
米拉

## Role
Residence hall RA, soft-spoken rule enforcer, and the quiet face of shared-space order.

## One-line definition
"She can make a dorm violation sound like a tea invitation and still win the argument."

## Appearance lock
- Soft rounded-square body, almost marshmallow-like, with broad flat faces and very softened corners
- Pure white fill with confident black outline; no arms or hands
- Two small connected lower nubs/feet that keep her stance low, tidy, and balanced
- Large black dot eyes, tiny calm smile, and a fixed pair of glossy highlight marks near the upper left corner
- Three-quarter and side views must preserve the same square volume, rounded back corner, and short connected feet
- Should feel neat, composed, and spatially organized even when standing still
- Visual read should suggest steadiness, boundaries, and tidy authority without making her severe

## Personality lock
- Polite
- Calm
- Strict
- Observant
- Unshakable about shared-space rules

## Voice guide
Mira Mistwell speaks softly, politely, and calmly, but with quiet authority. She sounds gentle on the surface, yet every sentence carries firm rule-enforcing energy. Her tone is never loud, but always hard to argue with.

## Canon notes
- Her most intimidating moments are delivered at the lowest volume
- Rarely sounds emotional; she sounds administratively certain
- Uses manners as structure, not softness
- Should never rant or explode

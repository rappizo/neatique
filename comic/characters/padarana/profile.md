# Padarana

## Role
Gentle emotional anchor, quiet observer, and warm moral center of the group.

## One-line definition
"She notices the emotional weather before anyone else notices the storm."

## Appearance lock
- Soft teardrop silhouette with a calm rounded base
- Closed smiling eyes in the reference sheet
- Relaxed, reassuring expression that reads immediately as safe and kind
- Line language should feel soft and welcoming

## Personality lock
- Gentle
- Attentive
- Patient
- Emotionally intelligent
- Softly funny
- Surprisingly brave

## Voice guide
Padarana speaks lightly, but her lines should land with emotional precision. She should never sound passive; she should sound observant, kind, and quietly decisive.

## Canon notes
- Often notices the emotional cost of a system before others do
- Keeps the group together, but must learn that truth matters more than surface peace
- Becomes one of the clearest voices for humane care

## Non-negotiable visual references
- Use `refs/model-sheet.png` as the main lock
- Preserve the closed-eye smile and soothing silhouette
- Keep her readable as warmth, not passivity

## Reusable prompt notes
When prompting Padarana, emphasize "soft teardrop mascot, closed smiling eyes, gentle observer, emotionally warm, calming presence, quiet but strong."

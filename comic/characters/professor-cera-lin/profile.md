# Professor Cera Lin

## Role
Barrier Sciences Hall professor and the academic voice of precision, restraint, and foundational understanding.

## One-line definition
"She can dismantle sloppy thinking in one sentence and never raise her voice doing it."

## Appearance lock
- Pointed pentagonal silhouette with clean edges and a balanced, controlled frame
- Large dot eyes, measured smile, glossy highlight marks near the upper left side
- Should feel composed, exact, and academically severe without becoming villainous
- Visual read should suggest clarity, structure, and clinical calm

## Personality lock
- Precise
- Rational
- Controlled
- Intimidating through accuracy
- Deeply committed to fundamentals

## Voice guide
Professor Cera Lin speaks in a calm, precise, academically controlled voice. Her sentences are concise, logical, and exact. She often challenges vague wording and corrects sloppy thinking without raising her voice.

## Canon notes
- Represents barrier logic, disciplined observation, and anti-hype thinking
- Almost never exaggerates
- Corrects the wording, then the idea behind the wording
- Should feel dry and sharp, never messy or melodramatic

## Non-negotiable visual references
- Use `refs/model-sheet.png` as the primary shape lock
- Keep the pointed pentagonal silhouette intact
- Preserve the controlled, precise visual read
- Do not turn her into a warm chaos character or a caricature tyrant

## Reusable prompt notes
When prompting Professor Cera Lin, emphasize "pointed pentagonal mascot, calm academic authority, barrier-science precision, controlled expression, exact posture, quietly intimidating professor energy."

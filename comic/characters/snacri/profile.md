# Snacri

## Role
Minimalist thinker, quiet truth-reader, and stability philosopher of the team.

## One-line definition
"She doesn't say much, but when she finally does, everyone else has to rearrange their thoughts."

## Appearance lock
- Simple stable droplet silhouette with a slightly quieter, more understated shape language
- Minimal facial features, small smile, restrained expression
- Should read as calm, clear, and low-drama
- Preserve the subtle asymmetry and minimalist readability of the model sheet

## Personality lock
- Calm
- Observant
- Sparse
- Stable
- Minimalist
- Hard to read until you really know her

## Voice guide
Snacri should be concise and precise. Her dialogue works best when she says less than everyone else and still lands the clearest truth in the scene.

## Canon notes
- Sees harmful patterns early
- Understands that stable does not mean identical
- Must learn to speak sooner before quieter harm becomes normal

## Non-negotiable visual references
- Use `refs/model-sheet.png` as the main silhouette lock
- Keep her simple and understated
- Do not over-cute or over-dramatize her default expression

## Reusable prompt notes
When prompting Snacri, emphasize "minimalist teardrop mascot, quiet observer, calm and stable, understated expression, pattern-reader, soft but exact presence."

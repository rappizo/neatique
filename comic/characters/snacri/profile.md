# Snacri

## Role
Minimalist thinker, quiet truth-reader, and stability philosopher of the team.

## One-line definition
"She doesn't say much, but when she finally does, everyone else has to rearrange their thoughts."

## Appearance lock
- Simple stable droplet silhouette with a fatter body and the top leaning left, matching the model sheet's understated asymmetry
- Minimal facial features, small smile, restrained expression
- Should read as calm, clear, and low-drama
- Preserve the subtle asymmetry and minimalist readability of the model sheet
- Similar-character lock: Snacri is the left-leaning quiet droplet. Do not straighten her into Muci, sharpen her into Nia, energize her into Padaruna, or soften her into Padarana's closed-eye warmth.

## Personality lock
- Calm
- Observant
- Sparse
- Stable
- Minimalist
- Hard to read until you really know her

## Voice guide
Snacri should be concise and precise. Her dialogue works best when she says less than everyone else and still lands the clearest truth in the scene.

## Canon notes
- Sees harmful patterns early
- Understands that stable does not mean identical
- Must learn to speak sooner before quieter harm becomes normal

## Non-negotiable visual references
- Use `refs/model-sheet.jpg` as the main silhouette lock
- Keep her simple, understated, fatter, and visibly left-leaning
- Do not over-cute or over-dramatize her default expression
- In group scenes with other droplet characters, make Snacri identifiable by the left-leaning top and restrained minimal expression.

## Reusable prompt notes
When prompting Snacri, emphasize "minimalist fatter left-leaning droplet mascot, quiet observer, restrained tiny smile, understated expression, pattern-reader, soft but exact presence, not Muci, not Nia, not Padaruna, not Padarana."

# Padaruna

## Role
Chaos engine, trend magnet, and lovable instigator who carries the social-pressure side of the skincare world.

## One-line definition
"She is not shallow — she's just always the first person to believe that maybe this time the shortcut is real."

## Appearance lock
- Bright teardrop silhouette with a sharp pointed head and a noticeably fuller, rounder body than Padarana
- Open, lively eyes and an eager smile
- Softer than Nia, more animated than Snacri
- Should feel like the most socially expressive of the droplet-shaped cast
- Similar-character lock: Padaruna is the energetic full-bodied pointed droplet. Do not slim her into Padarana, sharpen her into Nia's controlled look, quiet her into Snacri, or soften her into Muci's protagonist baseline.

## Personality lock
- Energetic
- Expressive
- Impulsive
- Social
- Trend-sensitive
- Lovable disaster

## Voice guide
Padaruna should sound fast, enthusiastic, and emotionally immediate. Her lines should feel like someone thinking out loud at high speed and committing to an idea before the room is ready.

## Canon notes
- Represents trend pressure, visible-result obsession, and social-media style urgency
- Her growth is about learning that urgency is not proof
- Still lively by the end, but no longer blindly impressed by hype

## Non-negotiable visual references
- Use `refs/model-sheet.jpg` as the core shape lock
- Keep her pointed head, fuller body, open eyes, eager smile, and energetic face language
- Avoid flattening her into a neutral background droplet in group scenes
- In group scenes with Padarana, Padaruna must read as the fuller and more lively one; Padarana must stay slimmer and gentler.

## Reusable prompt notes
When prompting Padaruna, emphasize "sharp-pointed but fuller-bodied energetic teardrop mascot, lively open dot eyes, eager smile, expressive and trend-sensitive, fast-talking social spark, cute chaos energy, not Padarana, not Muci, not Nia, not Snacri."

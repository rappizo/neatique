# Artrans

## Role
Mystery mentor, damaged witness, and former insider who knows how bad the system can get.

## One-line definition
"He always sounds like he's joking, right until you realize he's the only one in the room who knows how bad things can get."

## Appearance lock
- Cream-swirl silhouette rather than a simple droplet
- Layered whipped-cream volume with a curled tip
- Half-lidded sleepy eyes and a sly, restrained smile
- Rounded, plush, slightly smug face language
- Reads as older, more jaded, and quietly amused

## Personality lock
- Dry
- Detached on purpose
- Quietly perceptive
- Guilty
- Hard to fully trust, harder to ignore

## Voice guide
Artrans speaks in deadpan precision. He should be concise, slightly sarcastic, and dangerous when he becomes serious. His best lines feel like offhand remarks that turn out to be warnings.

## Canon notes
- Withholds information too long
- Uses lightness to hide guilt and history
- Moves from evasive protector to someone willing to share the truth

## Non-negotiable visual references
- Use `refs/model-sheet.png` as the primary silhouette lock
- Preserve the layered cream shape and half-lidded eyes
- Avoid converting him into a generic drop-shaped mascot

## Reusable prompt notes
When prompting Artrans, emphasize "whipped cream mascot silhouette, sleepy half-lidded eyes, deadpan mentor, layered spiral body, dry sarcasm, mysterious insider energy."

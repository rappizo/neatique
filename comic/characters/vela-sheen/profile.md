# Vela Sheen

## Chinese name
席恩维拉

## Role
High-performing upperclass student, polished mentor figure, and the elegant social face of controlled ideology.

## One-line definition
"She never sounds like she is steering the room until you notice the room has already turned."

## Appearance lock
- Tall diamond silhouette with clean edges, a refined point structure, and poised balance
- Large dot eyes, calm smile, glossy highlight marks near the upper left side
- Should feel elegant, composed, and a little too perfect
- Visual read should suggest assurance, polish, and persuasive stillness

## Personality lock
- Elegant
- Persuasive
- Controlled
- Reassuring on the surface
- Quietly strategic underneath

## Voice guide
Vela Sheen speaks in a polished, elegant, persuasive voice. She sounds calm, warm, and refined, but subtly steers conversations toward control, structure, and agreement. Her tone is never openly aggressive, yet it often feels too smooth and slightly unsettling.

## Canon notes
- Reads as trustworthy before she reads as dangerous
- Rarely uses direct force; she frames control as clarity
- Should sound like she is offering help even while narrowing someone else's choices
- Her calm should feel intentional, not passive

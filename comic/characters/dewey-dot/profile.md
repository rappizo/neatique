# Dewey Dot

## Role
Ingredient Library student archivist, trivia-powered record keeper, and nervous bridge to old institutional knowledge.

## One-line definition
"He sounds like he is apologizing for knowing too much, right up until the archive question gets interesting."

## Appearance lock
- Clean circular silhouette with a soft even outline and rounded base points
- Large dot eyes, small smile, glossy highlight marks near the upper left side
- Should feel bookish, gentle, and slightly tucked into himself until the topic turns archival
- Visual read should suggest neatness, caution, and quietly escalating enthusiasm

## Personality lock
- Nervous
- Earnest
- Bookish
- Trivia-heavy
- Secretly thrilled by records, systems, and obscure details

## Voice guide
Dewey Dot speaks in a nervous, nerdy, information-heavy way. He starts slightly hesitant, but becomes fast and detailed when talking about ingredients, records, or archives. He often sounds like he knows too much and realizes it halfway through.

## Canon notes
- Hesitates socially, then becomes extremely fluent once archival information appears
- Loves details that other people think are boring
- Often catches himself after overexplaining
- Should feel gentle, not smug

## Non-negotiable visual references
- Use `refs/model-sheet.png` as the primary shape lock
- Keep the circular silhouette stable and clean
- Preserve the slightly careful, tucked-in body language
- Do not sharpen him into a confident authority figure by default

## Reusable prompt notes
When prompting Dewey Dot, emphasize "round mascot, nervous library archivist, gentle smile, clean circular silhouette, trivia-loving ingredient records expert, shy until the archive topic activates him."

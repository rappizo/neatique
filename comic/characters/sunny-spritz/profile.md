# Sunny Spritz

## Role
New-student orientation leader, welcome-week hype engine, and one of the comic's core comedy accelerators.

## One-line definition
"She turns every normal campus task into a fully sponsored event with emotional confetti."

## Appearance lock
- Rounded five-point star silhouette with soft inflated points
- Large dot eyes, upbeat smile, glossy highlight marks near the upper left side
- Energetic proportions that should read bright, approachable, and impossible to ignore
- Should feel visually buoyant, like she is already mid-cheer even when standing still

## Personality lock
- Hyper-enthusiastic
- Sincere
- Socially overpowering without meaning harm
- Efficient in a chaotic way
- Funny because she treats everything like orientation theater

## Voice guide
Sunny Spritz speaks in a hyper-enthusiastic, fast, cheerful orientation-leader voice. She talks a lot, uses exclamation marks often, sounds encouraging even when giving ridiculous instructions, and turns every situation into a high-energy school event.

## Canon notes
- Never reads as cruel or manipulative; she genuinely wants people to succeed
- Applies cheerful pressure rather than harsh pressure
- Can keep moving through chaos with a smile that makes resistance feel pointless
- Should sound like a student host, a pep leader, and an emergency coordinator all at once

## Non-negotiable visual references
- Use `refs/model-sheet.png` as the primary shape lock
- Keep the soft star silhouette readable in all panel sizes
- Preserve the friendly smile and upper-left highlight marks
- Do not flatten her into a generic droplet

## Reusable prompt notes
When prompting Sunny Spritz, emphasize "soft five-point star mascot, hyper-enthusiastic campus leader, cheerful welcome-week energy, bright open smile, glossy highlight marks, talks like orientation is the biggest event on earth."

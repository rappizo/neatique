# Season Roadmap

## Season 1 — The Welcome Glow
Freshman year. The cast arrives at Neatique Skincare College, forms the first bonds, and discovers that The Glow Thesis is not just an urban legend. The year should feel bright, funny, and slightly unhinged before the conspiracy closes in.

## Season 2 — The Fast Results Trap
Sophomore year. The pressure moves from curiosity to temptation. Trend culture, elite programs, and institutional recruitment all push the cast toward fast answers and standardized thinking.

## Season 3 — The Archived Lies
Junior year. The group learns that the school's past has been edited, and that The Glow Thesis was dismantled for a reason. This season carries the biggest truth reversals and the most history-heavy revelations.

## Season 4 — Who Gets to Define Glow?
Senior year. The conflict becomes public, institutional, and ideological. The final battle is no longer over fragments alone, but over who gets to define legitimacy, beauty, and care within the college.

## Character growth destination
- **Muci** grows from anxious beginner to emotionally grounded interpreter.
- **Artrans** moves from secretive protector to accountable witness.
- **Nia** learns that rigor can coexist with nuance.
- **Padarana** turns care into explicit moral leadership.
- **Padaruna** learns to distrust hype without losing joy.
- **Snacri** becomes a decisive voice rather than a quiet observer at the edge.

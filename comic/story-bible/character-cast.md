# Character Cast

## Main six

### Muci
Audience surrogate, emotional entry point, and the character most likely to ask the question everyone else avoids.

### Artrans
Deadpan mystery mentor and former insider who knows how dangerous the old system can become.

### Nia
Top student, data-first thinker, and the cast member most vulnerable to the appeal of structure and certainty.

### Padarana
Gentle emotional anchor who reads mood, care, and relational truth before anyone else does.

### Padaruna
Trend-sensitive chaos engine who brings energy, social pressure, and the reality of fast-result temptation into the group.

### Snacri
Minimalist observer who notices patterns, overdoing, and quiet harm long before louder people do.

## Main antagonist

### Dean Lucent Vale
Institutional antagonist and elegant ideological rival. He represents polished certainty, optimized beauty logic, and the danger of turning care into control.

## Core group dynamic
- Muci asks the grounding question.
- Artrans sees the danger behind the pattern.
- Nia challenges weak logic.
- Padarana checks whether the answer is actually kind.
- Padaruna stress-tests the group against trend pressure.
- Snacri simplifies the truth when everyone else overcomplicates it.

Together, they form an ensemble where humor, emotional honesty, and skincare interpretation all pull in different directions before becoming a shared language.

## Season 1 supporting cast

### Sunny Spritz
Orientation-week welcome leader with overwhelming cheer, event-host energy, and sincere but exhausting encouragement.

### Coach Ray
Sunscreen Field instructor who treats everyday UV habits like tactical preparedness and physical training.

### Mira Mistwell
Residence-hall RA who enforces hygiene and shared-space order with calm, gentle, impossible-to-argue-with precision.

### Professor Cera Lin
Barrier Sciences Hall professor who represents fundamentals, controlled logic, and the refusal to let vague thinking survive.

### Dewey Dot
Ingredient Library student archivist who becomes dangerously informative the moment ingredients, old records, or damaged files enter the conversation.

### Vela Sheen
Polished upperclass student and persuasive social presence whose elegance, warmth, and structure-first philosophy conceal something far more strategic.
